'''Alys Clare''' is a pseudonym of '''Elizabeth Harris''', author of English [[historical whodunnit]] novels primarily set in medieval times, featuring the characters of Abbess Helewise and Josse d'Acquin.<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.detecs.org/helewise.html|title=Abbess Helewise & Josse d'Acquin (Alys Clare)|accessdate=16 March 2010| archiveurl= https://web.archive.org/web/20100228024443/http://detecs.org/helewise.html| archivedate= 28 February 2010 | url-status= live}}</ref> Brought up in the countryside close to where the Hawkenlye Novels are set, she went to school in [[Tonbridge]] and later studied English at the [[Keele University|University of Keele]].<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.severnhouse.com/author/Alys+Clare/9378|title=Alys Clare. Severn House website}}</ref> She has also published books as Elizabeth Harris.<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.fantasticfiction.co.uk/h/elizabeth-harris/|title=Elizabeth Harris - Fantastic Fiction}}</ref>
