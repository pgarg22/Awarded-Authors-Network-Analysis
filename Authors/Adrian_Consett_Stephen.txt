Major '''Adrian Consett Stephen''' [[Military Cross|MC]] (1894–1918) was an [[Australian]] artillery officer<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.awm.gov.au/collection/R1478525|title=Adrian Consett Stephen|website=www.awm.gov.au}}</ref> and [[playwright]].<ref>{{cite web|url=http://oa.anu.edu.au/obituary/stephen-adrian-consett-1280|title=Obituary – Adrian Consett Stephen – Obituaries Australia|website=oa.anu.edu.au}}</ref> He was awarded the [[Military Cross]] during [[World War I]].<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.stpauls.edu.au/home/our-rich-history/war-service/world-war-one/pauline-deaths-in-world-war-one/adrian-consett-stephen|title=St Paul's College » Adrian Consett Stephen|website=www.stpauls.edu.au}}</ref>
