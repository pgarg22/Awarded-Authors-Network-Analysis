{{short description|Hungarian writer and translator (born 1976)}}
'''Noémi Szécsi''' (born 1976) is a [[Hungary|Hungarian]] writer and translator. She was educated in [[Budapest]] and [[Helsinki]]. Her [[debut novel]] ''[[The Finno-Ugrian Vampire]]'' was published in 2002, and later translated into English. Her historical novel ''Communist Monte Cristo'' (2006) won the [[EU Prize for Literature]].<ref>[https://www.euprizeliterature.eu/authors/no%C3%A9mi-sz%C3%A9csi Profile]</ref>
