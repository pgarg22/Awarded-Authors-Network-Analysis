{{short description|American writer of science fiction (born 1961)}}
'''Richard Garfinkle''' (born 1961) is an American [[List of science fiction authors|writer of science fiction]].

He is best known as the author of ''[[Celestial Matters]]'', a novel published by [[Tor Books]], which won the [[Compton Crook Award]] in 1997.<ref name="compcrk">{{cite web |publisher=[[Baltimore Science Fiction Society]] |title=Compton Crook Award Winners |url=http://www.bsfs.org/bsfsccw.htm |accessdate=June 25, 2012}}</ref>

Garfinkle is a 1992 graduate of the [[Clarion West Writers Workshop|Clarion West Writers' Workshop]] and was nominated twice for the [[John W. Campbell Award for Best New Writer]].{{Citation needed|date=December 2011}}

He has taught at numerous writers' workshops at [[Windycon]], an annual Chicago-area [[science fiction convention]],{{Citation needed|date=December 2011}} and at Chicon 2000, the [[58th World Science Fiction Convention]], held in Chicago.<ref>[http://www.chicon.org/depts/writers.htm Chicago 2000 World Science Fiction Convention page]</ref>
