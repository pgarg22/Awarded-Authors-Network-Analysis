'''Jaclyn Reding''' (born March 23 in [[Ohio]], United States) is an American [[writer]] of [[Historical romance|historical]] [[Romance novel|romance]] [[novel]]s. She has been a [[Golden Quill Awards]] winner and the author of an Amazon.com's #1 bestseller.{{citation needed|date=January 2009}} She also is a [[National Readers' Choice Awards]] finalist and has received a nomination for the [[Romance Writers of America]] [[RITA Award]].

==Biography==
Reding first started writing in 1989,<ref name="Yost">{{cite news |last1=Yost |first1=Barbara |title=Romance writing still quickens pulses |url=https://www.newspapers.com/image/124027034/?terms=%22Jaclyn%2BReding%22 |accessdate=15 July 2019 |work=Arizona Republic |date=5 September 1997 |location=Phoenix, Arizona |page=8EV}}</ref> Her first book was rejected;<ref name="Yost"/> she sold her second book in 1992, and it was published in 1993.<ref>{{cite web|title=Highlanders and Pretenders - An interview with Bestselling Author Jaclyn Reding|url=http://www.gottawritenetwork.com/HistIV.htm|first=Linda|last=Morelli|accessdate=2007-10-09|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20090106162503/http://www.gottawritenetwork.com/HistIV.htm|archive-date=2009-01-06|url-status=dead}}</ref> Since her first novel she has since gone on to publish over a dozen award winning novels.

She has been a [[Golden Quill Awards]] winner and a [[National Readers' Choice Awards]] finalist. She has received a nomination for the [[Romance Writers of America|RITA Award]] for her book ''The Secret Gift''. She is also the author of the Amazon.com #1 bestseller, ''The Pretender'', a Georgian-era Scottish historical.

Reding lives in [[Massachusetts]] with her husband, Steven Reding, and their son.<ref name="Jones">{{cite news |last1=Jones |first1=Bill |title=Passion for writing fuels romance novels |url=https://www.newspapers.com/image/124086936/?terms=%22Jaclyn%2BReding%22 |accessdate=15 July 2019 |work=Arizona Republic |date=26 July 1996 |location=Phoenix, Arizona |pages=1GC, 6GC}}</ref><ref name=JaclynReding.com>{{cite web |title=Jaclyn Reding's Biography |first= |last= |work=JaclynReding.com |url=http://www.jaclynreding.com/bio.html |date= |accessdate=2007-09-03}}</ref>
