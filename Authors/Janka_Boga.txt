'''Janka Boga Dénesné''' (31 January 1886 – 4 October 1963) was a [[Hungary|Hungarian]] writer and teacher.

Born in Gyergyóújfalu (now [[Suseni, Harghita|Suseni]], [[Romania]]) in 1886,<ref>{{cite news|title=Ma történt|url=http://www.hirosnaptar.hu/index.php?oldal=cikk&cikk=ma_tortent_-_2016__januar_31_|work=Hírös Naptár|access-date=17 July 2017|language=hu|date=31 January 2016}}</ref> Boga worked as a teacher in [[Kecskemét]] and retired in 1952. In 1920, she started publishing her writings in several local publications, and [[Szeged]] was the place for the first performances of her theatre plays.<ref>{{cite news|title=Dénesné, Boga Janka|url=http://www.arcanum.hu/hu/online-kiadvanyok/Lexikonok-magyar-eletrajzi-lexikon-7428D/d-750BB/denesne-boga-janka-7519F/|access-date=17 July 2017|work=Magyar életrajzi lexikon|language=hu}}</ref>

==Works==
