'''Álvaro Torres-Calderón''' (born 1975) is a Peruvian [[poet]].

== Biography ==
Torres-Calderón was born in Peru in 1975. He has been recognized<ref>{{cite news|last=Torres-Calderón|first=Alvaro|title=El auge poético|url=http://www.elperuano.com.pe/edicion/noticia.aspx?key=4YSN9RcQb50=|accessdate=2 August 2011|newspaper=Diario Oficial El Peruano|date=26 September 2010|language=Spanish}}</ref> as member of a new generation of poets in his country. His book of poems ''Claroscuro''<ref>{{cite news|last=Torres-Calderón|first=Alvaro|title=Presentan poemario Claroscuro|url=http://www.rpp.com.pe/2010-10-01-presentan-poemario-claroscuro-de-alvaro-torres-calderon-noticia_299577.html|accessdate=2 August 2011|newspaper=RPP Noticias|date=2 August 2011|language=Spanish}}</ref> talks about his childhood, adolescence and questions about life. It has references to magical beings such as angels, demons and fairies.

He is also a lawyer and studied in his native country and in Spain. Torres-Calderón received his doctorate in literature and [[Romance languages]] in the United States.

Torres-Calderón has participated in the publication of the book ''Alejo Carpentier Ante la Crítica'', Caracas: Monte Avila, 2005, with his article "Alejo Carpentier y el Hombre Fronterizo: Una Constante en el Reino de Este Mundo". He also published in the U.S. a set of poems called ''Spells'' at the Stonepile Writers Anthology from the [[North Georgia College & State University]] Press in November 2010. He has contributed to the Peruvian literary journal ''Tinta Expresa'' with the article "Nación, Identidad y Frontera en la Prosa de Clorinda Matto de Turner". Torres-Calderón has participated in several national and international literary conferences as well as interviews in Peruvian newspapers and a radio presentation of his creative works (December 2010).<ref>{{cite web|last=Torres-Calderón|first=Alvaro|title=Peruanos en el exterior|url=http://radio.rpp.com.pe/peruanosenelexterior/los-peruanos-que-viven-en-el-extranjero-destacan-por-sus-logros/#more-2602|publisher=RPP Noticias|accessdate=2 August 2011|language=Spanish}}</ref> He has performed and assisted in many theatrical works at college and university level and performed musically at the same level. His interests include Integration Law, Latin-American female writers of the 19th century,<ref>{{cite web|last=Torres-Calderón|first=Alvaro|title=Diario La Primera|url=http://www.diariolaprimeraperu.com/online/especial/ellas-escriben_77207.html|work=Ellas escriben|publisher=La Primera|accessdate=2 August 2011|language=Spanish}}</ref> [[José Martí]], and Latin American civilization.
