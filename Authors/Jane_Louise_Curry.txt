'''Jane Louise Curry''', born September 24, 1932 in [[East Liverpool]], [[Ohio]],<ref>{{cite web|url=https://ohiocenterforthebook.org/2018/08/16/curry-jane-louise|title=Curry, Jane Louise|website=Ohio Center for the Book|date=August 16, 2018|accessdate=January 26, 2020}}</ref> is a prolific author of adventure, fantasy, mystery, time travel, and American Indian tales for older children and teenagers. She has written 39 books.<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.fantasticfiction.com/c/jane-louise-curry|title=Jane Louise Curry|website=Fantastic Fiction|accessdate=January 26, 2020}}</ref>

Her novels include the famous Abaloc series, set in the magical landscape of the [[Ohio Valley]] and surrounding regions in contemporary, medieval, and prehistoric times.
