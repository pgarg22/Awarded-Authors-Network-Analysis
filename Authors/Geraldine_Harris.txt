'''Geraldine Harris''' (born 1951), aka '''Geraldine Harris Pinch''', is an author (of both fiction and non-fiction) and [[Egyptologist]].{{efn|She uses her married name, Pinch, for academic Egyptology publications and her maiden name, Harris, for material written for children.[http://errol.oclc.org/laf/nr94-21187.html]}} She is a member of the [[Oriental Institute, Oxford|Faculty of Oriental Studies]] at the [[University of Oxford]].

Her works include the [[Seven Citadels]] quartet and numerous information text books about [[Egypt]].
