'''Gyula Juhász''' (4 April 1883, [[Szeged]] &ndash; 6 April 1937, Szeged) was a [[Hungarian people|Hungarian]] poet, who was awarded the [[Baumgarten Prize]].

His first poems were published in {{Lang|hu|[[Szegedi Napló]]}} in 1899. Between 1902 and 1906 he was a student of the [[Eötvös Loránd University|University of Budapest]], where he met [[Mihály Babits]] and [[Dezső Kosztolányi]]. He committed suicide in 1937, after multiple failed attempts.
