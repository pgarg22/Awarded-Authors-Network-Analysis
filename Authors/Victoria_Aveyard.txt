'''Victoria Aveyard''' (born July 27, 1990) is an American writer of young adult and fantasy fiction and screenplays.<ref>{{cite web|title='Red Queen' is familiar fantasy fare|url=https://www.usatoday.com/story/life/books/2015/02/17/red-queen/23497451/|work=USA Today|date=February 17, 2015}}</ref> She is known for her fantasy novel ''[[Red Queen (novel)|Red Queen]]''.<ref>{{cite web|title=Victoria Aveyard: I'm so pleased that badass ladies are considered a 'hot trend'|url=https://www.theguardian.com/childrens-books-site/2015/jul/01/victoria-aveyard-red-queen-interview|work=The Guardian|date=July 1, 2015}}</ref> Aveyard wrote the novel a year after graduating from [[University of Southern California]]'s screenwriting program in 2012.<ref>{{cite web|title=Elizabeth Banks in Talks to Direct YA Fantasy 'Red Queen'|url=http://www.hollywoodreporter.com/heat-vision/elizabeth-banks-talks-direct-ya-801884|work=[[The Hollywood Reporter]]}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|title='Red Queen' Writer Reveals Cover, Talks Inspirations and Splurges|url=http://www.hollywoodreporter.com/heat-vision/red-queen-writer-reveals-cover-715795|work=The Hollywood Reporter}}</ref> Sony Pictures teamed up with her to write spec screenplay ''Eternal''.<ref>{{cite web|title=Sony Acquires Spec 'Eternal' for 'Divergent' Producer and Michael Costigan|url=https://variety.com/2014/film/news/sony-eternal-divergent-michael-costigan-1201068320/|work=Variety|year=2014}}</ref>
