'''Unorthodox Australian Poet''' is the pen name of [[Western Australia]]n born [[poet]], Garry W. Gosney. He has also published works as ''The Perth Desperado''.

==Life==
Born 19 October 1955, Garry William Gosney of [[Mount Lawley, Western Australia]] a suburb of [[Perth, Western Australia|Perth]], is an only child to Jean and William Gosney. He suffers from [[dyslexia]],<ref name="web1">[http://www.publishedauthors.net/cgi-bin/perlfect/search/search.pl?q=Christmas&showurl=/garrygosney/bio.htm l''www.publishedauthors.net''] Biography</ref> and as an adult was diagnosed with [[ADHD]] and [[ADD]].

At the age of five he was sent to the [[ Anglican Church]] Swanlea Boarding Hostel, followed by Middle Swan State School, De La Salle Catholic day School and [[Castledare Boys' Home|Castledare Catholic Boarding College and Clontarf Boy's Town Boarding School]].<ref name="web1"/><ref name="web2">[http://www.freewebs.com/garrygosney/ ''www.freewebs.com/garrygosney/'']</ref>

In 1998 a serious vehicle accident left Gosney with severe [[nerve damage]] and pain.<ref name="web2"/>

He has four sons and a daughter by his first wife, Karen. He has since divorced his second wife.
