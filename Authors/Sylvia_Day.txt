'''Sylvia June Day''' (born March 11, 1973) is a [[Japanese American]] [[writer]] of [[romance novel]]s. She also writes under the pseudonyms '''S.J. Day''' and '''Livia Dare'''. She is a number one bestselling author in 28 countries.<ref>{{cite web|title=SylviaDay.com|url=http://www.sylviaday.com/about/|accessdate=23 April 2015}}</ref>

== Career ==
Day writes romance novels, speculative/paranormal fiction, historical fiction, and futuristic fiction (under the pseudonym Livia Dare). She has also published fiction under the pseudonym S. J. Day, and non-fiction.

She is the co-founder of [[Passionate Ink]], a special interest chapter of Romance Writers of America (RWA),<ref>{{cite web|title=Passionate Ink Founders |url=http://www.passionateink.org/founders/ |publisher=Passionate Ink |accessdate=2007-12-26}}</ref> and served on RWA's Board of Directors from 2009-13. She was the 22nd President of RWA.<ref>{{cite web |title=About RWA: Board of Directors |url=http://www.rwanational.org/cs/about_rwa/board_of_directors |publisher=[[Romance Writers of America]] |access-date=2009-11-06 |url-status=dead |archive-url=https://archive.is/20120917160855/http://www.rwanational.org/cs/about_rwa/board_of_directors |archive-date=2012-09-17 |df= }}</ref> Day presently serves on the Authors Guild Board of Directors.<ref>{{cite web|title=Who We Are: Board of Directors |url=https://www.authorsguild.org/who-we-are/board-directors/ |publisher=[[Authors Guild]] |accessdate=2017-07-30}}</ref>

She presents workshops for writing groups and has been a speaker at events such as the RT Booklovers Convention, Romance Writers of America's National Convention, and [[San Diego Comic-Con International|Comic-Con]].<ref>{{cite web|title=Open Book Society|url=http://openbooksociety.com/article/autumns-comic-con-adventure-sylvia-day-breaking-dawn-e-l-james-fifty-shades-of-gray-and-more-obs-speaks-out/|website=Open Book Society|accessdate=27 April 2015}}</ref>

In March 2013, [[Harlequin Enterprises]] and [[Hearst Corporation]] announced<ref>{{cite web|date=March 12, 2013 |title=#1 New York Times Bestselling Author Sylvia Day inks seven-figure deal with Harlequin to launch Cosmo Red Hot Reads eBooks |url=http://www.prnewswire.com/news-releases/1-new-york-times-bestselling-author-sylvia-day-inks-seven-figure-deal-with-harlequin-to-launch-cosmo-red-hot-reads-ebooks-197305741.html |publisher=PR NewsWire |accessdate=2013-03-13}}</ref> the signing of Day to a seven-figure contract to write two [[novellas]] to launch "''Cosmo'' Red Hot Reads from Harlequin," a new collaboration between the publisher and communications giant.

In June 2013, [[Penguin Group|Penguin USA]] agreed on an eight-figure deal for two more "Crossfire" books, with [[Penguin Books|Penguin UK]] acquiring UK and [[Commonwealth of Nations|Commonwealth]] rights for an additional seven-figures.<ref>{{cite web|date=June 26, 2013 |title=Writer Sylvia Day secures 'eight-figure deal' for a pair of erotic novels |url=http://www.thetimes.co.uk/tto/arts/books/article3800408.ece |work=[[The Times]] |accessdate=2014-01-26}}</ref>

In January 2014, [[Macmillan Publishers|Macmillan]]'s [[St. Martin's Press]] announced a two-book, eight-figure agreement with Day for a new "Blacklist" series.<ref>{{cite web|date=January 16, 2014 |title=St. Martin's Press Acquires #1 New York Times Bestselling Author Sylvia Day |url=http://www.prnewswire.com/news-releases/st-martins-press-acquires-1-new-york-times-bestselling-author-sylvia-day-240488161.html |publisher=PR NewsWire |accessdate=2014-01-26}}</ref> Penguin UK acquired UK and Commonwealth rights to the series<ref>{{cite web|date=January 14, 2014 |title=Penguin UK keeps hold of Sylvia Day |url=http://www.thebookseller.com/news/penguin-uk-keeps-hold-sylvia-day.html |publisher=[[The Bookseller]] |accessdate=2014-01-26}}</ref> for an additional seven-figures.<ref>{{cite web|date=January 15, 2014 |title=Romance Novelist Wins Big Valentine |url=https://www.nytimes.com/2014/01/16/business/media/crossfire-author-gets-eight-figures-for-a-new-series.html |work=[[The New York Times]] |accessdate=2014-01-26}}</ref>

In April 2019, [[Amazon Publishing]] announced a deal for a new [[novella]] from Day for seven figures.<ref>{{cite web|date=April 29, 2019 |title= Montlake Romance to Publish International Bestselling Author Sylvia Day |url= https://www.businesswire.com/news/home/20190429005736/en/Montlake-Romance-to-Publish-International-Bestselling-Author-Sylvia-Day |publisher=Business Wire |accessdate=2021-10-21}}</ref>

=== Crossfire ===

Day's Crossfire series has 13 million English-language copies in print and international rights licensed in over 40 territories as of January 2014.<ref>{{cite web|date=January 16, 2014 |title=St. Martin's Press Acquires #1 New York Times Bestselling Author Sylvia Day |url=http://www.prnewswire.com/news-releases/st-martins-press-acquires-1-new-york-times-bestselling-author-sylvia-day-240488161.html |publisher=PR NewsWire |accessdate=2014-01-26}}</ref>

''[[Bared to You]]'' was #4 on the [[Amazon.com]]'s list of top 10 best-selling books of 2012,<ref>{{cite web|title=Amazon.com Announces Best-Selling Books of 2012 |url=http://phx.corporate-ir.net/phoenix.zhtml?c=176060&p=irol-newsArticle&ID=1767368&highlight= |publisher=Amazon.com |accessdate=2012-12-14}}</ref> #5 on iTunes' Top Ten Books of the Year,<ref>{{cite web|title=iTunes Top Ten Books of the Year Also Dominated by Erotic Romance |url=http://www.digitalbookworld.com/2012/itunes-top-ten-books-of-the-year-also-dominated-by-erotic-romance/ |publisher=digitalbookworld.com |accessdate=2012-12-18}}</ref> and #7 on [[Nielsen BookScan|Bookscan]]'s Top 10 Print Book Sales of 2012 – Adult Fiction.<ref>{{cite web|title=Nielsen Tops of 2012: Entertainment |url=http://blog.nielsen.com/nielsenwire/media_entertainment/nielsen-tops-of-2012-entertainment/ |publisher=Nielsen |accessdate=2012-12-18}}</ref> ''Bared to You'' spent forty-five weeks on [[The New York Times]] trade paperback bestseller list and sixty-seven weeks on the [[USA Today]] bestseller list.<ref>{{cite web|date=April 29, 2019 |title= Montlake Romance to Publish International Bestselling Author Sylvia Day |url= https://www.businesswire.com/news/home/20190429005736/en/Montlake-Romance-to-Publish-International-Bestselling-Author-Sylvia-Day |publisher=Business Wire |accessdate=2021-10-21}}</ref>

The Crossfire series was acquired by [[Lionsgate Television]] Group for [[television adaptation]],<ref>{{cite web|title=Lionsgate Acquires Television Rights to the #1 New York Times Bestselling Series Crossfire by Award Winning Author Sylvia Day|url=http://www.prnewswire.com/news-releases/lionsgate-acquires-television-rights-to-the-1-new-york-times-bestselling-series-crossfire-by-award-winning-author-sylvia-day-218352681.html| publisher=[[PR Newswire]]|accessdate=5 August 2013}}</ref> but Day declined a third renewal of the option and the rights have reverted to her.<ref>{{cite web|title=Will there be a Crossfire® motion picture or television series?|url=http://www.sylviaday.com/faq/crossfire-motion-picture-television-series/|accessdate=16 August 2018}}</ref>

=== ''Beyond Words'' ===
In September 2015, Day launched the digital lifestyle magazine ''Beyond Words'', which publishes daily articles covering travel, entertainment, style, wellness, and philanthropy.

==Media==

In April 2013, HeroesAndHeartbreakers.com<ref>{{cite web|last=Frampton|first=Megan|title=Sylvia Day's Crossfire Series Optioned for Television!|url=http://www.heroesandheartbreakers.com/blogs/2013/04/sylvia-days-crossfire-series-optioned-for-television| publisher=HeroesAndHearbreakers.com|accessdate=13 April 2013}}</ref> broke the news that Day's Crossfire series had been [[Option (filmmaking)|optioned]] for television adaptation. [[Lions Gate Entertainment]] secured the rights.<ref>{{cite web|title=FAQs - SylviaDay.com|url=http://www.sylviaday.com/faq/#crossfire|publisher=sylviaday.com|accessdate=13 April 2013}}</ref> Kevin Beggs, President of the [[Lionsgate Television]] Group, confirmed the acquisition on August 5, 2013 in a press release.<ref>{{cite web|title=Lionsgate Acquires Television Rights to the #1 New York Times Bestselling Series Crossfire by Award Winning Author Sylvia Day|url=http://www.prnewswire.com/news-releases/lionsgate-acquires-television-rights-to-the-1-new-york-times-bestselling-series-crossfire-by-award-winning-author-sylvia-day-218352681.html| publisher=PR Newswire|accessdate=5 August 2013}}</ref> Lionsgate TV Executive Vice President Chris Selak, who was to oversee development for the studio, said, "The Crossfire series is an incredible property and it is a thrill to bring it to Lionsgate. Sylvia has created an enduring, sexy and edgy story, and we're looking forward to working with her to create a show that both excites and connects with audiences as her books have done." However, Day declined a third renewal of the option and the rights have reverted to her.<ref>{{cite web|title=Will there be a Crossfire® motion picture or television series?|url=http://www.sylviaday.com/faq/crossfire-motion-picture-television-series/|accessdate=16 August 2018}}</ref>

In June 2017, [[Startup company|startup]] [[Streaming media|streaming]] [[entertainment]] company Passionflix began [[Filmmaking|production]] of Day's ''Afterburn/Aftershock'' [[film adaptation]]. [[Principal photography]] [[Wrap (filmmaking)|concluded]] on July 29, 2017. The film debuted in November 2017.<ref>{{cite web|title=Afterburn/Aftershock (2017)|url=https://www.imdb.com/title/tt7116248/| publisher=IMDB|accessdate=30 July 2017}}</ref>

''Beyond Words: Sylvia Day'', a [[documentary]] covering the world tour supporting the release of Day’s ''One with You'', was released on October 9, 2018.<ref>{{cite web|title=Beyond Words: Sylvia Day (2018)|url=https://www.imdb.com/title/tt9102842/| publisher=IMDB|accessdate=21 October 2018}}</ref>
