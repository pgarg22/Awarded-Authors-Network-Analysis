'''Geoff Nelder''' is a British freelance [[editing|editor]] and author. He has written both fiction and non-fiction, and his research in the field of air pollution and climate won him a fellowship with the [[Royal Meteorological Society]].<ref name="met1"/> Nelder's fictional work falls in the genres of [[science fiction]], [[fantasy]] and [[Thriller (genre)|thrillers]], and he is known for his sci-fi series ''ARIA'', which won him a ''Preditors & Editors Award for best science fiction novel''.<ref name="bfsa2"/> Nelder also published the sci-fi magazine ''Escape Velocity'', which launched in 2009. Prior to moving to writing as his primary occupation, Nelder has worked as a teacher at [[Queens Park High School]] for 26 years.<ref name="pos3"/>

In 1975 Nelder became a life member of the British Vegan Society.
In 2009 Nelder became a publisher for ''BeWrite Books'', an independent publisher.<ref name="che4"/>
