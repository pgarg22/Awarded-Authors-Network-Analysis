'''Count László Listi de Köpcsény et Jánosháza''' (''List, Listi, Listh, Listhy, Liszti, Liszty, Listius''; 1628 – February 16, 1662) was a [[Hungarian people|Hungarian]] poet.

Listi was executed for [[counterfeiting]] coins in 1662.
