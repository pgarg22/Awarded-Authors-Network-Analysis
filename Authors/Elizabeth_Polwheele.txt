== ''The Frolicks, or The Lawyer Cheated'' ==
On October 11-12 of 2021, second year students of the Shakespeare and Performance program at Mary Baldwin University in Staunton, Virginia<ref>{{Cite web|last=link|first=Get|last2=Facebook|last3=Twitter|last4=Pinterest|last5=Email|last6=Apps|first6=Other|title=News: Hedgepig Ensemble and Partners "Expand the Canon" Again Beginning Sep. 13|url=https://www.thinkingtheaternyc.com/2021/09/news-hedgepig-ensemble-and-partners.html|access-date=2021-10-14|language=en}}</ref> premiered the first full length production of ''The Frolicks.'' The show was produced by the program and was directed by Sara Hymes and Gregory Jon Phelps, two members of the Hedgepig Ensemble located in Brooklyn, New York. Hedgepig worked closely with the production's publicity team as the play was selected for Hedgepig's 2021 "Expand the Canon" list.
