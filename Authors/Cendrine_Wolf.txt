'''Cendrine Wolf''' (born 1969 in Colmar) is a [[French people|French]] children's writer, known for co-writing the ''[[Oksa Pollock]]'' series with [[Anne Plichota]].<ref>{{cite news|title=Oksa Pollock: The Last Hope by Anne Plichota and Cendrine Wolf - review|url=https://www.theguardian.com/childrens-books-site/2011/nov/07/oksa-pollock-plichota-wolf-review?newsfeed=true|publisher=Guardian|accessdate=29 March 2013|location=London|date=7 November 2011}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|title=articulars Anne Plichota, Cendrine Wolf|url=http://translate.googleusercontent.com/translate_c?depth=1&ei=cBNVUfPeN4y70QHNh4GwCA&hl=en&prev=/search%3Fq%3DCendrine%2BWolf%26hl%3Den&rurl=translate.google.com&sl=de&u=http://www.spiegel.de/spiegel/print/d-77962994.html&usg=ALkJrhgSB9-zqLKrbqUkw8rBeFdUMdWYFw|publisher=Der Spiegel|accessdate=29 March 2013}}</ref> In 2012 the two announced that they would be collaborating on a new series centering on the character of [[Susan Hopper]].
