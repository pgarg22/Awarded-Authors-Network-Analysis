'''Annette Curtis Klause''' (born June 20, 1953) is an English-American writer and librarian, specializing in young adult fiction. She is currently a children's materials selector for Montgomery County Public Libraries in [[Montgomery County, Maryland]]. Born in [[Bristol]], England, she now lives in [[Hyattsville, Maryland]] with her husband Mark and their cats. She holds a Bachelor of Arts degree in English literature and a [[Master of Library and Information Science|Master of Library Science]] degree from the [[University of Maryland, College Park]].

*''[[Blood and Chocolate (novel)|Blood and Chocolate]]'' (1997, Delacorte)
*''[[Freaks: Alive on the Inside]]'' (2006, [[Simon & Schuster|Margaret K. McElderry]])

===Other publications===
Klause contributed [[book review]]s to the ''[[School Library Journal]]'' from 1982 through 1994.
