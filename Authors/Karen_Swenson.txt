{{Short description|American poet and journalist}}
'''Karen Swenson''' (born July 29, 1936 [[New York City]]) is an American [[poet]] and [[journalist]].

==Life==
She grew up in [[Chappaqua, New York]], and studied at [[Barnard College]] and [[New York University]].<ref>{{Cite web | url=http://www.poets.org/poet.php/prmPID/966 |title = About Karen Swenson &#124; Academy of American Poets}}</ref>

Swenson has been Poet-in-Residence at [[Skidmore College]], the [[University of Idaho]], [[University of Denver]], [[Clark University]], [[Scripps College]] and [[Barnard College]].  She taught at [[City College, New York]].<ref>{{Cite web | url=http://www.albany.edu/writers-inst/webpages4/archives/swenson.html | title=New York State Writers Institute - Karen Swenson}}</ref>

Her work has appeared in ''The New York Times'',<ref>{{cite news| url=https://query.nytimes.com/search/sitesearch?query=KAREN+SWENSON&more=date_all | work=The New York Times}}</ref> ''The [[Beloit Poetry Journal]]'',<ref>{{Cite web |url=http://www.bpj.org/index/S.html |title=Archived copy |access-date=2009-09-18 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20180222103810/http://www.bpj.org/index/S.html |archive-date=2018-02-22 |url-status=dead }}</ref> ''Paris Review'',<ref>{{cite web |url=http://www.theparisreview.org/viewissue.php/prmIID/73 |title=Archived copy |accessdate=2009-12-14 |url-status=dead |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20090708231203/http://www.theparisreview.org/viewissue.php/prmIID/73 |archivedate=2009-07-08 }}</ref> ''American Poetry Review'', "Saturday Review", and "The New Yorker".
