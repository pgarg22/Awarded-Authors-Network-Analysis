'''Jan Coffey''' is a joint [[pen name]] for the American [[writer]]s '''James A. McGoldrick''' and '''Nikoo Kafi McGoldrick''', a married couple.<ref>{{cite news |last1=Debelle |first1=Penny |title=Passion pays: cashing in on the romance novel |url=https://www.adelaidenow.com.au/lifestyle/sa-weekend/passion-pays-how-romantic-fiction-is-driving-billions-of-dollars-in-sales--and-making-the-world-better/news-story/96b9c7b42de25959ca7234a6d7468bce |work=[[The Advertiser (Adelaide)|The Advertiser]] |date=7 October 2016 |language=en}}</ref><ref name="McGoldrick">{{Citation | title = Official home page | url = http://www.maymcgoldrick.com | access-date = 2008-01-04}}</ref> They also write [[historical]]-[[romance novels]] as '''May McGoldrick''' and other [[suspense]]-[[romance novels]] as '''Nicole Cody'''. As '''Nikoo McGoldrick & James A. McGoldrick''', they also wrote the book ''Marriage of Minds''.

==Biography==
James A. McGoldrick and Nikoo Kafi met in 1979 in [[Stonington, Connecticut]], and married the following year. He has a PhD in sixteenth-century British literature and she was a manufacturing engineer.<ref name="Bio">{{Citation | title = Authors's Official Biography | url = http://www.jancoffey.com/about.html | access-date = 2008-01-04}}</ref> They have two children and reside in California.
