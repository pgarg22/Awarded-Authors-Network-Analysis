'''Marcin Baran''' (born 1963 in [[Kraków]]) is a [[Poland|Polish]] [[poet]] and [[journalist]]. He has a degree in [[Polonistics]] from the [[Jagiellonian University]]. He is one of the Polish poets who published their verses in the magazine ''[[bruLion]]'' (sometimes spelled ''brulion''). 

One of the main themes of his poetry is [[woman]], for him the [[symbol]] of deathlessness. His poems have been translated into [[German language|German]], [[Slovak language|Slovak]] and [[Czech language|Czech]].
