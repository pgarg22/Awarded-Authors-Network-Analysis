'''Helene Deborah Wecker''' is an American writer, author of the Mythopoeic Award-winning historical fantasy novel ''[[The Golem and the Jinni]]'' and its sequel. She writes as '''Helene Wecker'''.

==Biography==
Wecker was born and raised in [[Libertyville, Illinois|Libertyville]], a northern suburb of [[Chicago]].<ref name="webbio">Wecker, Helene. "[http://www.helenewecker.com/about-helene-wecker About the Author]."</ref><ref name="ISFDB">{{isfdb name|id=186293|name=Helene Wecker}}</ref><ref name="HC">[https://www.harpercollins.com/blogs/authors/helene-wecker Author entry for Helene Wecker] at harpercollins.com.</ref> After graduating with a Bachelor's Degree in English from [[Carleton College]], she worked in marketing and communications in [[Minneapolis]] and [[Seattle]] before "deciding to return to her first love, fiction writing."<ref name="webbio"/> Moving to [[New York City|New York]], she received a Master of Fine Arts degree from [[Columbia University]].<ref name="webbio"/><ref name="HC"/> Wecker currently resides near [[San Francisco]] with her husband and daughter.<ref name="webbio"/><ref name="HC"/>

==Literary career==
Wecker's first novel, ''[[The Golem and the Jinni]]'', was published by HarperCollins in April 2013.<ref name="ISFDB"/><ref name="webbio"/> A sequel with the working title ''The Iron Season'' was initially projected for release in 2018.<ref name="webbio"/> It has been published on 8 June 2021 under the title ''The Hidden Palace''.<ref name="A">[https://www.amazon.com/s?k=The+Hidden+Palace%3A+A+Tale+of+the+Golem+and+the+Jinni&ref=nb_sb_noss_2 Amazon.com entry for ''The Hidden Palace''].</ref>

==Awards==
''The Golem and the Djinni'' won the 2014 [[Mythopoeic Awards|Mythopoeic Fantasy Award for Adult Literature]], was nominated for the 2014 [[Nebula Award for Best Novel]] and was a finalist for the 2013 [[List of Otherwise Award winners|James Tiptree Jr. Award]]. It placed second in the 2014 [[Locus Award for Best First Novel|Locus Poll Award for Best First Novel]] and the 2013 Goodreads Award for the Goodreads Debut Author Award, and third in the 2013 Goodreads Award for Fantasy.<ref name="ISFDB"/><ref name="webbio"/>
