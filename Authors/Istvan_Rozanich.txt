'''Istvan Rozanich''' (1912–1984) was a [[Hungary|Hungarian]] [[poet]] and, while living in [[exile]] in [[Venezuela]] during the second half of his life, [[newspaper]] [[editing|editor]]. He published three volumes of poetry during his lifetime, and a collection in English, 'Selected Poems' was published in [[Hungary]] in 2005.
