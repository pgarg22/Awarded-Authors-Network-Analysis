'''Anthony Butts''' (born July 28, 1969 in [[Detroit]], [[Michigan]]) is an American [[poet]].

==Life==
He graduated from [[Wayne State University]] with a bachelor's degree, from [[Western Michigan University]],<ref>{{Cite web|url=http://www.wmich.edu/wmu/news/2004/0404/0304-357.html|title = WMU News - Alumnus Anthony Butts earns prestigious writing award}}</ref> with an MFA, and [[University of Missouri]] with a PhD.<ref>http://arc.psu.edu/databases/pennsylvania-black-writers/the-writers/anthony-butts/life-and-work{{dead link|date=October 2016 |bot=InternetArchiveBot |fix-attempted=yes }}</ref>

He taught at the [[University of Dayton]] as well as [[Carnegie Mellon University]].<ref>{{Cite web|url=http://www.pw.org/content/anthony_butts_1|title = Anthony Butts}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web|url=http://www.cmu.edu/cmnews/011205/011205_voices.html|title=Carnegie Mellon News Online Edition: December 5, 2001}}</ref>
He was married to journalist Leah Samuel (b. 1970) from 2004-2009.
