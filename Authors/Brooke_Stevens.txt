'''Brooke Stevens''' is an American novelist. His first novel, ''The Circus of the Earth and the Air'', was a nominee for the [[Barnes & Noble]] Discover Great New Writers Award in 1994 and a finalist for the [[World Fantasy Award—Novel|World Fantasy Award]] in 1995. He has published two subsequent novels, not works of fantasy, and has taught creative writing at [[Sarah Lawrence College]]. His work has been translated into French, German, and Japanese. He lives in [[Kent, Connecticut]].
