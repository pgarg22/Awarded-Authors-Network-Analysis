'''Catherine Banner''' (born 1989) is a British author, born in [[Cambridge]], England and living in [[Turin]], Italy. She gained international attention with her first book, ''[[The Eyes of a King]]'', which she began writing when she was fourteen and still a school student.<ref>{{Cite news|title = Catherine Banner, 19, poised to be the next J.K. Rowling | work = [[Daily News (New York)]] | date = 2008-06-04 | url = http://www.nydailynews.com/entertainment/2008/06/04/2008-06-04_catherine_banner_19_poised_to_be_the_nex.html | accessdate = 2008-06-15 | first=Nicole | last=Carter}}</ref><ref>{{Cite news| title = UK teen author the new Rowling? | work = [[The Times of India]] | date = 2008-06-09 | url = http://timesofindia.indiatimes.com/World/UK_teen_author_the_new_Rowling/articleshow/3111996.cms | accessdate = 2008-06-16 | archiveurl =https://web.archive.org/web/20080612014444/http://timesofindia.indiatimes.com/World/UK_teen_author_the_new_Rowling/articleshow/3111996.cms | archivedate = 2008-06-12}}</ref><ref>{{Cite news| title = Author, 19, Who Wrote First Book at 14 Hailed as Next J.K. Rowling | work = [[Fox News]] | date = 2008-06-05 | url = http://www.foxnews.com/story/0,2933,363470,00.html | accessdate = 2008-06-16}}</ref>

Banner studied English at [[Fitzwilliam College, Cambridge]], before moving to County Durham where she worked as a secondary school teacher. She has published a trilogy of young adult novels, ''[[The Last Descendants]]''. Her debut adult novel, The House at the Edge of Night, was published in 2016. Her work is translated into 22 languages. She lives in Turin, Italy, with her husband.<ref>{{Cite web|url=http://www.catherine-banner.com/about.html|title=About Carherine Banner}}</ref>
