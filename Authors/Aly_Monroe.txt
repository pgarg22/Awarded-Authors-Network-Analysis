'''Aly Monroe''' is a British writer of [[Historical fiction|historical]] [[Thriller (genre)|thrillers]] set in the 1940s. She was brought up in [[Purley, London|Purley]],<ref>{{Cite web|title = Aly Monroe: Setting the Place for Icelight|url = http://www.shotsmag.co.uk/feature_view.aspx?FEATURE_ID=181|website = www.shotsmag.co.uk|access-date = 2015-10-03}}</ref> England. She has spent a large part of her life in Spain, where she worked as a teacher, translator and [[voice-over]] artist.<ref>{{Cite web|title = About Aly Monroe|url = http://www.alymonroe.com/aly-monroe.htm|website = www.alymonroe.com|access-date = 2015-10-03}}</ref><ref name=":0">{{Cite journal|url = http://ic.galegroup.com/ic/bic1/BiographiesDetailsPage/BiographiesDetailsWindow?failOverType=&query=&prodId=BIC1&windowstate=normal&contentModules=&display-query=&mode=view&displayGroupName=Biographies&limiter=&currPage=&disableHighlighting=false&displayGroups=&sortBy=&search_within_results=&p=BIC1&action=e&catId=&activityType=&scanId=&documentId=GALE%7CH1000204188&source=Bookmark&u=dclib_main&jsid=799f39b5bf5364790ad4e70c7346018a|title = Aly Monroe|date = 2012|journal = Contemporary Authors Online|access-date = 2015-10-03|location = Detroit}}</ref> She is the author of the Peter Cotton series of historical novels, including ''Icelight'' which won the 2012 [[Ellis Peters Historical Award]].<ref>{{Cite web|title = Icelight – The Crime Writers' Association|url = http://thecwa.co.uk/icelight/|website = thecwa.co.uk|access-date = 2015-10-03}}</ref>

==Bibliography==
Monroe's series character is Peter Cotton who works for the British intelligence service.<ref name=":0" /> Her first novel, ''The Maze of Cadiz'', is set in Spain, where Monroe lived for many years. The second novel, ''Washington Shadow'', was set in Washington, DC and shortlisted for the [[Crime Writers' Association|CWA]] [[CWA Historical Dagger|Ellis Peters Historical Fiction Award]].<ref>{{Cite web|title = Fiction Book Review: Washington Shadow by Aly Monroe|url = http://www.publishersweekly.com/978-0-7195-2093-8|access-date = 2015-10-04|date = 1 November 2015|website = Publishers Weekly}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web|title = Washington Shadow – The Crime Writers' Association|url = http://thecwa.co.uk/washington-shadow/|website = thecwa.co.uk|access-date = 2015-10-04}}</ref> The third novel of the series, ''Icelight'', won the CWA Ellis Peters Historical Award.
