'''Alison Brackenbury''' (born 1953 [[Gainsborough, Lincolnshire]]<ref name=pa>[https://www.poetryarchive.org/poet/alison-brackenbury Alison Brackenbury | poetryarchive.org] Retrieved 2018-02-14.</ref> ) is a British poet.

==Life==
After studying English at [[St Hugh's College, Oxford]]<ref name=pa/> she now lives in [[Gloucestershire]].<ref>{{Cite web|url = http://www.the-chimaera.com/October2007/Various/Brackenbury.html|title = The Chimaera, October 2007: Alison Brackenbury|website = www.the-chimaera.com|access-date = 2016-04-11|archive-url = https://web.archive.org/web/20160303185106/http://www.the-chimaera.com/October2007/Various/Brackenbury.html|archive-date = 3 March 2016|url-status=dead|df = dmy-all}}</ref> Her work has appeared in the ''Kenyon Review'',<ref>{{cite journal|jstor=4337249 |title=Alison Brackenbury |journal=The Kenyon Review |date=1995 |volume=17 |issue=3/4 |pages=77â€“78|last1=Brackenbury |first1=Alison }}</ref> ''Ploughshares''.<ref>{{Cite web|url = http://www.pshares.org/authors/author-detail.cfm?authorID=185|title = Read By Author {{!}} Ploughshares|website = www.pshares.org|access-date = 2016-04-11}}</ref> ''Stand'',<ref>{{Cite book|url = https://books.google.com/books?id=dIhZAAAAIAAJ|title = Stand|language = en}}</ref>
