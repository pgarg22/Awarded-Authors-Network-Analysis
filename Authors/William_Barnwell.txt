{{Short description|American writer}}
'''William Barnwell''' (born 1943) is an American author of science fiction, and of other genres. He has written the ''Blessing Trilogy'', set in a future Ireland, and which has [[metaphysics|metaphysical]] content. It was praised by [[Andre Norton]], while [[Peter Nicholls (writer)|Peter Nicholls]] stated that Barnwell wrote like "a member of a [[pseudoscience]] [[cult]]." In 1985, Barnwell wrote ''Book of the Romes'', a [[prequel]] to the ''Blessing Trilogy'', a historical novel with science fiction/fantasy elements.
