'''Melissa James Gibson''' is a Canadian-born [[playwright]] based in New York.

==Life==
The child of former [[British Columbia Liberal Party|BC Liberal]] [[Member of the Legislative Assembly|MLA]] [[Gordon Gibson]] and journalist Valerie Gibson, Melissa James Gibson grew up in North Vancouver.<ref name=TP>[https://theprovince.com/entertainment/This+perfect+return+Vancouver+born+playwright/4066326/story.html Theprovince.com]{{dead link|date=May 2013}}</ref> She graduated from [[Columbia University]] and from the [[Yale School of Drama]] with an M.F.A. in Playwriting. After graduating, she worked as a college counselor at the exclusive arts-oriented [[Saint Ann's School (Brooklyn)|Saint Ann's School]] in [[Brooklyn Heights]], where her daughter, Celia Frey, graduated in 2017 and her son, Griffin Frey, is a current sophomore.<ref>Del Signore, John. [https://web.archive.org/web/20171106035447/http://gothamist.com/2012/12/26/melissa_james_gibson_interview.php "Playwright Melissa James Gibson Discusses ''What Rhymes With America''"], ''[[Gothamist]]'', December 26, 2012. "I was a college counselor for many years at a private school in Brooklyn, St. Ann's. It's a wonderful school, really cool school. I was there pretty much since graduate school, other than two fellowship years."</ref>  She is working on commissions for the [[La Jolla Playhouse]] and The Adirondack Theatre Festival. She has received fellowships from the Jerome Foundation and the [[MacDowell Colony]].<ref>{{cite web|url=http://newdramatists.org/melissa_james_gibson.htm# |title=Alumni Playwrights |publisher=New Dramatists |access-date=2013-05-22 |url-status=dead |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20090902135143/http://www.newdramatists.org/melissa_james_gibson.htm |archive-date=September 2, 2009}}</ref>
[[The New York Times]] theatre critic [[Charles Isherwood]] wrote that, with her play ''This'', Gibson “graduates into the theatrical big leagues with this beautifully conceived, confidently executed and wholly accessible work.”
<ref name=TP/>

She subsequently wrote episodes for ''[[The Americans]]'' and ''[[House of Cards (American TV series)|House of Cards]]'', before joining Endeavor Content in an overall deal.<ref>{{Cite web|last=Zorrilla|first=Mónica Marie|date=2021-11-04|title=‘The Americans’ Writer Melissa James Gibson Inks Two-Year Overall Deal With Endeavor Content|url=https://variety.com/2021/tv/news/melissa-james-gibson-endeavor-content-1235105050/|access-date=2021-11-06|website=Variety|language=en-US}}</ref>
