'''Vojin Jelić'''{{Pronunciation needed}} (November 27, 1921 – December 19, 2004) was a [[Croatian Serb]] writer and poet. He was born in [[Knin]] in 1921, and finished [[Gymnasium (school)|gymnasium]] in [[Šibenik]], and went on to study in [[Belgrade]], [[Prague]], and [[Zagreb]]. He wrote about Serbian culture and stories from the Knin region and [[Dalmatian Zagora]]. He died in Zagreb.
