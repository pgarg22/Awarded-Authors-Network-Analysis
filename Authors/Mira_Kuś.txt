'''Mira Kuś''' (born 1958 in Gorlice, Poland) is a contemporary [[Polish poets|Polish poet]]. She lives in [[Kraków]] and is a journalist and member of the [[Polish Writers Association]].

==Works==
She graduated in [[physics]] at [[Jagiellonian University]] and she often writes science articles for the press.

Translations of some of her poems have appeared in foreign newspapers and magazines in [[Germany]], [[United States|U.S.]], [[Sweden]], [[Bulgaria]], [[Slovenia]], [[Ukraine]], [[Hungary]], [[Spain]] etc. including ''[[The Southern Review]]'' and ''[[Mid-American Review]]''.
