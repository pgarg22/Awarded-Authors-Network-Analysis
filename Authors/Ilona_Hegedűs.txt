'''Ilona Hegedűs''' is a Hungarian writer of [[science fiction]], [[fantasy]] and [[horror poetry]], writing in [[English language|English]], who has written ''[[Unearthly Companion]]'' (2005), a book of speculative poetry with poems nominated for [[Muses Prize]] and [[James B. Baker Award]]. She was the editor of the ''[[European Reader]]'' magazine (2006–2010).
She is also well known as a book reviewer.
