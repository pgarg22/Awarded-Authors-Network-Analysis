'''Paul S. Kemp''' is a fantasy [[author]] most known for his [[Forgotten Realms]] novels.

==Education==
Kemp is a graduate of the [[University of Michigan–Dearborn]].<ref name="wizardsbio">{{cite web|url=http://ww2.wizards.com/Books/Wizards/Bios/default.aspx?doc=Paulkemp|title=Paul Kemp|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20090224012620/http://ww2.wizards.com/Books/Wizards/Bios/default.aspx?doc=Paulkemp|archivedate=February 24, 2009}}</ref> Kemp is a 2000 graduate of the [[University of Michigan]] School of Law.<ref name=KempTwitter20130304>Kemp mentioned that he attended University of Michigan's law school as part of a story he told [https://twitter.com/Paulskemp/status/308626094423162880 on Twitter].</ref>

==Career==
Some examples of his work include ''[[Resurrection (Forgotten Realms)|Resurrection]]'', the final installation of ''[[War of the Spider Queen]]'' series. He is also a strong defender of [[shared world]] fiction.

==Personal life==
Kemp is a ''[[Dungeons & Dragons]]'' player.<ref name=krug20110525>{{cite web | first=Kurt Anthony | last=Krug | date=May 25, 2011 | title=Grosse Pointe Park Resident Authors Star Wars Novels | work=Grosse Pointe Patch | url=http://grossepointe.patch.com/articles/grosse-pointe-park-resident-authors-star-wars-novels | accessdate=2011-06-06 }}</ref> He also practices corporate law in Detroit.<ref name="wizardsbio"/> Kemp lives in [[Grosse Pointe, Michigan]], with his wife Jennifer.<ref name="wizardsbio"/>
