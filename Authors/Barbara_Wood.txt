'''Barbara Wood''' (born January 30, 1947, in [[Warrington]] ([[Lancashire, England]]) is an American writer of [[historical romance|historical]] [[romance novels]].  Her family moved to [[California]], where she grew up. In 2002, she received the [[Corine Literature Prize]].
