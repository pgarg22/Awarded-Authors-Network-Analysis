'''Zbigniew Morsztyn''' (Morstin, Morstyn) (ca. 1628 &ndash; December 13, 1689) was a [[Poland|Polish]] [[poet]].

Morsztyn was born in [[Kraków]]. For nine years (1648-1657) he was in the army, and fought against the [[Swedish Empire|Swedes]] and [[Tsardom of Russia|Russians]] during the [[Northern Wars]]. His most celebrated work was religious poetry, contrasting with the style of his cousin, [[Jan Andrzej Morsztyn]].

Morsztyn was a member of a [[Christianity|Christian]] sect called the [[Polish Brethren]], which existed from 1562 to 1658. Due to religious persecution <ref>{{cite book |url=https://books.google.com/books?id=p7U8AAAAIAAJ&q=+polish+brethren+1668&pg=PA209|title=A Republic of nobles: studies in Polish history to 1864|first=Janusz|last=Tazbir|access-date=2009-09-28 |publisher=J. K. Fedorowicz|year=2008 |page=208|isbn=9780521240932}}</ref> in Poland Morsztyn fled in 1662 to the [[Duchy of Prussia]] (since 1618 in personal union with the [[Margraviate of Brandenburg]]) <ref name=Bahlke/> where he became a Ducal Councillor of [[Frederick William, Elector of Brandenburg]].<ref>{{cite book|first=Andreas |last=Kossert|title=Masuren, Ostpreußens vergessener Süden|year=2001|pages=71|isbn=3-570-55006-0}}</ref> With the help of Radziwill he  leased [[Stara Rudówka]] (Rudowken)<ref name=Bahlke>{{cite book |url=https://books.google.com/books?id=ROxa3Y92mz0C&q=Zbigniew+Morsztyn&pg=PA130|title=Glaubensflüchtlinge  |access-date=2009-09-06 |publisher=Joachim Bahlcke|year=2008 |page=130 |language=de|isbn=9783825866686  }}</ref><ref>[https://books.google.de/books?id=ROxa3Y92mz0C&pg=PA130&dq=Zbigniew+Morsztyn&lr=&as_brr=3#v=onepage&q=Zbigniew%20Morstin%20Rudowken%20bei%20Rhein&f=false Morstin, Morstyn leased Rudowken bei Rhein)]</ref> In 1669 Morsztyn became an administrator of the estates owned by the duchess [[Ludwika Karolina Radziwiłł]].<ref>Tadeusz Oracki, Słownik biograficzny Warmii, Prus Książęcych i Ziemi Malborskiej od połowy XV do końca XVIII wieku, Tom 2, Pojezierze, 1988, s. 104 [https://books.google.com/books?id=7REaAAAAIAAJ&q=Radziwi%C5%82%C5%82+morsztyn+1669+Karolina&dq=Radziwi%C5%82%C5%82+morsztyn+1669+Karolina&lr=&hl=pl Google Books]</ref> He died in [[Königsberg]] in 1689, and was buried in Stara Rudówka the following year.
