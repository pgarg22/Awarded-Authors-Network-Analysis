'''Addepalli Ramamohana Rao''' was a noted Telugu poet and literary critic from India.<ref name="TOI">[http://timesofindia.indiatimes.com/city/rajahmundry/Poet-Ramamohana-Rao-no-more/articleshow/50570993.cms   Poet Ramamohana Rao no more - Times of India]</ref>

==Early life==
He was born in [[Chintalaguntapalem]] village in [[Machilipatnam]] of [[Andhra Pradesh]], India on 6 September 1936.<ref name="TOI"/>

==Works==
He penned 13 compilations of poetry, 25 books on literary criticism and 600 books on various genres.<ref name="TOI"/>

==Awards==
*Praja Kavi<ref name="TOI"/>
