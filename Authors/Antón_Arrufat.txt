'''Antón Arrufat Mrad''' (born 14 August 1935, [[Santiago de Cuba]], [[Cuba]]) is a [[Cuban people|Cuban]] dramatist, novelist, short story writer, poet and essayist.

Arrufat is of mixed [[Catalan people|Catalan]] and [[Lebanese people|Lebanese]] parentage. At the age of 11, he moved with his family from Santiago de Cuba to Havana. He studied philology at the [[University of Havana]]. His first book appeared in 1962, a collection of his early poems. He won the [[Premio Nacional de Literatura de Cuba]] in 2000.<ref>{{cite web |url=http://www.cubaliteraria.cu/autor/anton_arrufat/biografia.html |title=Antón Arrufat.
