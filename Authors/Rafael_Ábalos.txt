 }}</ref> The children's [[Fantasy literature|fantasy novel]] was about a boy finding a mysterious [[amulet]] in [[France]] who becomes a focus of a "centuries-old mission" to enlighten humanity.<ref name=twsFebL27/> According to a review in ''[[Publishers Weekly]]'', Ábalos "blends the grand-scale storytelling prowess and epic quest element of Tolkien’s ''[[Lord of the Rings]]'' with the cryptographic intrigue of Dan Brown’s ''[[The Da Vinci Code]]''", and gave it a positive review.<ref name=twsFebL27/> The book was published by ''[[Random House]]''.<ref name=twsFebL28>{{cite magazine
