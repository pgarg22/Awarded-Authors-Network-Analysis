'''Karen Miller''' is an Australian writer. She is best known for ''[[The Innocent Mage]]'', the first book in her duology ''Kingmaker, Kingbreaker''. 

==Biography==
Miller was born in [[Vancouver]], [[British Columbia]], Canada and moved to Australia at the age of two.<ref name="KM B">{{cite web | url = http://karenmiller.net/index.cfm?page=7 | title = About the author | publisher = Karen Miller | date = | accessdate = 2008-06-05}}</ref> After graduating from the [[University of Technology, Sydney]] she moved to England for three years before moving back to Australia. Along with being a novelist she has written and directed plays for her local theatre group.<ref name="KM B"/>

In 2005 Miller's [[debut novel|first novel]] was released entitled ''[[The Innocent Mage]]''. This was the first novel in the ''Kingmaker, Kingbreaker'' series and was followed shortly after by ''Innocence Lost''. ''The Innocent Mage'' was widely acclaimed on its release in Australia and was a finalist in the 2005 [[Aurealis Awards]] fantasy division.<ref name="KM TIM">{{cite web|url=http://www.locusmag.com/SFAwards/Db/Aurealis2006.html |title=2005 Aurealis Awards |publisher=[[Locus Magazine]] |accessdate=2008-06-05 |url-status=dead |archiveurl=https://www.webcitation.org/5ogoCwEjX?url=http://www.locusmag.com/SFAwards/Db/Aurealis2006.html |archivedate=2010-04-02 }}</ref> Since then she has written several other novels, including two for [[Fandemonium (publisher)|Fandemonium]]'s ''[[Stargate literature#The Fandemonium novels|Stargate SG-1]]'' series. In 2007, she was shortlisted for the [[James Tiptree, Jr. Award]] for her novels ''Empress of Mijak'' and ''The Riven Kingdom''.<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.tiptree.org/|
title=James Tiptree, Jr. Literary Award Council)}}</ref>

Miller also writes under the pseudonym K. E. Mills,<ref name="KM FAQ">{{cite web | url = http://karenmiller.net/index.cfm?page=6 | title = Frequently asked questions | publisher = Karen Miller | date = | accessdate = 2008-06-05}}</ref> releasing the first novel in the ''Rogue Agent'' series, ''[[The Accidental Sorcerer]]'', under this pen name.
