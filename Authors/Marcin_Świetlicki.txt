'''Marcin Świetlicki''' (born 24 December 1961) is a [[Poland|Polish]] [[poet]], writer, and musician. He lives and works in [[Kraków]], Poland.

Świetlicki was born in [[Piaski, Świdnik County|Piaski]], near [[Lublin]], [[Poland]].  He studied Polish Literature at the [[Jagiellonian University]] in Kraków, where he has been living since 1980.  He worked as an editor at the Tygodnik Powszechny weekly until 2004.  Beside his extensive publications and readings as a poet, he also performs as an actor, and heads the band [[Świetliki]] (Fireflies).  Świetlicki has won various prizes and awards for his poetry, including the 1996 [[Kościelski Award]].
