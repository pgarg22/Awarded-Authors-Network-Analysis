'''Tade Thompson''' is a British-born Nigerian psychiatrist best known for his science fiction novels.

==Life and career==
Thompson was born in [[London]] to Yoruba parents. His family left the [[United Kingdom]] for [[Nigeria]] around 1976, when Thompson was about seven.  He grew up in Nigeria, where he studied [[medicine]] and [[social anthropology]]. He went on to specialise in [[psychiatry]]. He returned to the UK in 1998, where he has remained except for a year spent working in [[Samoa]]. He now lives on the south coast of England.<ref name="agency" /><ref name="Macmillan" /><ref name="SH" />

His novels and short stories have been critically received. Thompson was a [[Nommo Award]] and a [[Kitschies]] Golden Tentacle Award winner. He was a [[John W.
