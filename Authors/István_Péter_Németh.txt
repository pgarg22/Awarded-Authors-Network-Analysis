'''István Péter Németh''' ([[Hungarian language|Hungarian]]: Németh István Péter) is a [[Hungary|Hungarian]] [[poet]] and [[literary historian]].

==Early life==
Németh was born on 8 March 1960 in [[Tapolca]], [[Hungary]].<ref>{{cite web |url = http://www.nemethistvanpeter.hu/ |title = Németh István Péter - official website |access-date = 2010-08-13 |archive-url = https://web.archive.org/web/20100504134533/http://www.nemethistvanpeter.hu/ |archive-date = 2010-05-04 |url-status = dead }}</ref>
