'''Georges Baal''' (born '''György Balassa'''; 17 May 1938 – 6 December 2013) was a [[Hungarian people|Hungarian]] writer, theatrologist, actor, director and psychologist.

Georges Baal died of a [[Myocardial infarction|heart attack]] on 6 December 2013, aged 75, in [[Saint-Mandé]], [[Paris]], [[France]], where he had lived since 1956.<ref>[http://szinhaz.hu/kulfold/54630-elhunyt-georges-baal-pszichoanalitikus-rendezo-szinhazi-szakember Elhunyt Georges Baal] {{in lang|hu}}</ref>
