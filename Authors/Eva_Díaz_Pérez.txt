'''Eva Díaz Pérez''' (born 1971) is a Spanish journalist and writer. She is also a teacher of {{ill|cultural journalism|es|Periodismo cultural}} at the EUSA University Center and a lecturer. She has received the Andalusian Journalism Award, and in July 2019 she was appointed director of the {{ill|Centro Andaluz de las Letras|es}} (CAL).

==Biography==
Eva Díaz Pérez has a [[Licentiate (degree)#Spain|licentiate]] in information sciences from the [[University of Seville]]. Her first job as a journalist was at the now-defunct ''[[Diario 16]]''. She worked at ''[[El Mundo (Spain)|El Mundo]]'' for more than 20 years as an editor specializing in cultural issues. She has been a contributor to the newspapers ''[[ABC (newspaper)|ABC]]'' and ''[[El País]]'' and the magazines ''Mercurio'' and ''Andalucía en la Historia''.<ref name=gatos>{{Cite news |url=https://www.elmundo.es/papel/cultura/2019/03/12/5c86112f21efa0f91b8b4673.html |title=Eva Díaz Pérez: 'Los políticos creen que la cultura es un asunto de cuatro gatos' |trans-title=Eva Díaz Pérez: 'Politicians Believe That Culture is a Matter of Four Cats' |first=Silvia |last=Moreno |work=[[El Mundo (Spain)|El Mundo]] |language=Spanish |date=2019-03-12 |access-date=2019-07-29}}</ref><ref>{{Cite news |url=https://elpais.com/autor/eva_diaz_perez/a |title=Eva Díaz Pérez |work=[[El País]] |language=Spanish |access-date=2019-07-29}}</ref><ref>{{Cite news |url=https://sevilla.abc.es/autor/eva-diaz-perez-2114/ |title=Eva Díaz Pérez |work=[[ABC (newspaper)|ABC]] |language=Spanish |access-date=2019-07-29}}</ref>

She began her literary career in 2001 with the publication of the satirical book ''El polvo del camino. El libro maldito del Rocío''. In 2003 and 2004 she was a finalist for the [[National Journalism Award (Spain)|National Journalism Award]]. In 2005, the José Manuel Lara Foundation published her [[historical novel]] ''Memoria de cenizas'', for which she received the 2008 Unamuno "Friend of the Protestants" Award from ''{{ill|Protestante Digital|es}}'', "for her important contribution to the recovery of the Protestant [[historical memory]]."<ref>{{Cite web |url=http://protestantedigital.com/espana/22610/Eva_Diaz_Premio_Unamuno08_por_rescatar_la_memoria_historica_protestante |title=Eva Díaz, Premio Unamuno '08 por rescatar la memoria histórica protestante |trans-title=Eva Díaz, '08 Unamuno Award for Rescuing Protestant Historical Memory |work=Protestante Digital |location=Madrid |language=Spanish |date=2009-01-18 |access-date=2019-07-29}}</ref>

Also in 2005, she was a finalist for the [[Premio Nadal]] with her work ''El Club de la Memoria''. In 2006, the Lara Foundation published her ''Hijos del Mediodía'', which won [[Radio y Televisión de Andalucía|RTVA]]'s El Público Radio and Televisión Award for Andalusian Culture.<ref>{{Cite web |url=http://www.juntadeandalucia.es/cultura/caletras/autores/eva-diaz-perez |title=Eva Díaz Pérez |publisher=Centro Andaluz de las Letras |language=Spanish |access-date=2019-07-29}}</ref>

In 2011, Díaz published her fourth novel, ''El sonámbulo de Verdún'', and in 2013, ''Adriático''. The latter won the 7th {{ill|Málaga Novel Award|es|Premio Málaga de Novela}}, as well as the Andalusian Critics Award in May 2014.<ref>{{Cite news |url=https://www.elmundo.es/andalucia/2014/05/19/537a400e22601d7b118b457a.html |title=Eva Díaz Pérez recoge el Premio de la Crítica por su obra 'Adriático' |trans-title=Eva Díaz Pérez Receives the Critics Award for Her Work 'Adriático' |work=[[El Mundo (Spain)|El Munco]] |location=Seville |language=Spanish |date=2014-05-19 |access-date=2019-07-29}}</ref> In 2013 she received a literary career award from the Seville Book Fair. Journalism awards she has won include the Francisco Valdés,<ref>{{Cite news |url=https://www.elmundo.es/andalucia/2015/05/10/554f366322601de3028b4571.html |title=Eva Díaz Pérez gana el Premio de Periodismo 'Francisco Valdés' |trans-title=Eva Díaz Pérez Wins the 'Francisco Valdés' Journalism Award |work=[[El Mundo (Spain)|El Mundo]] |location=Seville |language=Spanish |date=2015-05-10 |access-date=2019-07-29}}</ref> Unicaja,<ref>{{Cite news |url=https://www.elmundo.es/elmundo/2013/10/22/andalucia_malaga/1382456086.html |title=Eva Díaz Pérez recoge el Premio Unicaja de Artículos Periodísticos |trans-title=Eva Díaz Pérez Receives the Unicaja Award for Journalistic Articles |work=[[El Mundo (Spain)|El Mundo]] |location=Cádiz |language=Spanish |date=2013-10-22 |access-date=2019-07-29}}</ref> University of Seville, City of Málaga, and City of Huelva.<ref name=dirigirá>{{Cite news |url=https://www.elmundo.es/andalucia/2019/07/25/5d397d0a21efa041348b467b.html |title=La escritora y periodista Eva Díaz Pérez dirigirá el Centro Andaluz de las Letras |trans-title=The Writer and Journalist Eva Díaz Pérez to Direct the Centro Andaluz de las Letras |first=Nerea |last=Larrinaga Bidegain |work=[[El Mundo (Spain)|El Mundo]] |location=Seville |language=Spanish |date=2019-07-25 |access-date=2019-07-29}}</ref>

In 2017 she published the novel ''El color de los ángeles'' about Baroque painter [[Bartolomé Esteban Murillo]].

In non-fiction, Díaz is known for her essays ''La Andalucía del exilio'' (2008) and ''Travesías históricas: viajeros que contaron el mundo'' (2017), as well as the literary guide ''Sevilla, un retrato literario'' (2011). She is the co-author of the 2005 biography ''Salvador Távora. El sentimiento trágico de Andalucía''.

She teaches literature courses at the {{ill|Centro Andaluz de las Letras|es|lt=CAL}} and the [[International University of Andalucía]] (UNIA), and gives lectures at foreign universities. She also teaches {{ill|cultural journalism|es|Periodismo cultural}} classes at the EUSA University Center, attached to the University of Seville, where she has worked since 2013.<ref>{{Cite news |url=https://www.lavozdigital.es/cadiz/el-puerto/lvdi-diaz-perez-literatura-y-buen-periodismo-rescatan-curiosidad-gente-201808212122_noticia.html |title=Eva Díaz Pérez: 'La Literatura y el buen Periodismo rescatan la curiosidad de la gente' |trans-title=Eva Díaz Pérez: 'Literature and Good Journalism Rescue People's Curiosity' |first=Verónica |last=Sánchez |work=La Voz de Cádiz |location=El Puerto de Santa María |language=Spanish |date=2018-08-21 |access-date=2019-07-29}}</ref>

In 2018 she won the Andalusian Journalism Award for "Google time", a series of cultural articles published in the magazine ''Andalucía en la Historia''.<ref>{{Cite web |url=https://www.centrodeestudiosandaluces.es/noticias/eva-diaz-perez-premio-andalucia-de-periodismo-por-sus-cronicas-del-pasado-publicadas-en-andalucia-en-la-historia |title=Eva Díaz Pérez, Premio Andalucía de Periodismo por sus crónicas del pasado publicadas en 'Andalucía en la Historia' |trans-title=Eva Díaz Pérez, Andalusian Journalism Award for Her Chronicles of the Past Published in 'Andalucía en la Historia' |publisher=Centro de Estudios Andaluces |language=Spanish |date=2018-11-16 |access-date=2019-07-29}}</ref><ref>{{Cite news |url=https://www.elmundo.es/andalucia/2018/11/14/5bec448ae2704ed7a18b4692.html |title=La periodista y escritora Eva Díaz Pérez, premio Andalucía de Periodismo |trans-title=The Journalist and Writer Eva Díaz Pérez, Andalusian Journalism Award |work=[[El Mundo (Spain)|El Mundo]] |agency=Europa Press |location=Seville |language=Spanish |date=2018-11-14 |access-date=2019-07-29}}</ref> She was the curator of the Año Murillo, Seville's multidisciplinary event to commemorate the 400th anniversary of the painter's birth.<ref name=gatos/>

In July 2019, Díaz won a competition convened by the {{ill|Ministry of Culture and Historical Heritage of the Regional Government of Andalusia|es|Consejería de Cultura y Patrimonio Histórico de la Junta de Andalucía|lt=Andalusian Ministry of Culture and Historical Heritage}} to determine the successor of {{ill|Juan José Téllez Rubio|es}} as CAL director.<ref name=dirigirá/><ref>{{Cite news |url=https://sevilla.abc.es/cultura/sevi-diaz-perez-gana-concurso-para-nueva-directora-centro-andaluz-letras-201907241951_noticia.html |title=Eva Díaz Pérez gana el concurso para ser la nueva directora del Centro Andaluz de las Letras |trans-title=Eva Díaz Pérez Wins the Competition to Be the New Director of the Centro Andaluz de las Letras |first=J. |last=Morillo |work=[[ABC (newspaper)|ABC]] |location=Seville |language=Spanish |date=2019-07-25 |access-date=2019-07-29}}</ref>
