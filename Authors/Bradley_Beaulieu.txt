'''Bradley P. Beaulieu''' is an American author of [[epic fantasy]]. He is best known for his series ''The Lays of Anuskaya'', which he finished in 2013 with the publication of ''The Flames of Shadam Khoreh''. In 2015 Beaulieu published ''[[Twelve Kings in Sharakhai]]'', the first novel in his series ''[[The Song of the Shattered Sands]]''.

Beaulieu started writing in college and has since been nominated for several awards, including the Gemmell Morningstar Award for ''The Winds of Khalakovo''.<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://quillings.com/about/|title = About – Bradley P. Beaulieu}}</ref> He dedicated himself to the craft, learning under the guidance of writers like [[Nancy Kress]], [[Joe Haldeman]], [[Tim Powers]], [[Holly Black]], [[Michael Swanwick]], [[Kij Johnson]], and many more.<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://quillings.com/about/|title = About – Bradley P. Beaulieu}}</ref>
