'''Björn Theodor Björnsson''' (September 3, 1922 – August 25, 2007) was an [[Iceland]]ic writer born in [[Reykjavík]]. He attended the [[University of London]], [[University of Edinburgh]], and the [[University of Copenhagen]]. He was President of the Icelandic writers union for a time. He was married to an artist, [[Ásgerður Búadóttir]], and has written several popular [[historical novel]]s.
