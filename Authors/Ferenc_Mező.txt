'''Ferenc Mező''' (Pölöskefő, also known as ''Grünfeld''; March 13, 1885 – November 21, 1961) was a [[Hungary|Hungarian]] [[poet]]. He was born in [[Pölöskefő]], [[Zala County]] and died in [[Budapest]]. In 1928, he won a gold medal in the art competitions of the Olympic Games for his "History of the Olympic Games".<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.olympedia.org/athletes/920775 |title=Ferenc Mező |work=Olympedia |accessdate=25 July 2020}}</ref>
