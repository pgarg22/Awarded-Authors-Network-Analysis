'''Enrique Moya''' (born 1958 in [[Caracas]], [[Venezuela]]) is a [[Venezuelan people|Venezuelan]] [[poet]], fiction writer, literary translator, essayist and critic of music and literature.<ref name="hidra">{{cite book|last=Marcotrigiano Luna|first=Miguel |title=Las Voces de la Hidra: La Poesía Venezolana de Los Años 90|url=https://books.google.com/books?id=yW_UrBGxQtIC&lpg=PA314&dq=Enrique%20Moya%20Venezuela&pg=PA314#v=onepage&q=Enrique%20Moya%20Venezuela&f=false|accessdate=2012-11-23|date=January 2002|publisher=Mucuglifo|language=Spanish|isbn=980-6351-58-4|page=314}}</ref><ref name="qarrtsiluni -1">{{cite web|url=http://qarrtsiluni.com/2011/01/31/two-poems-from-the-spanish-by-enrique-moya/|title=Two poems from the Spanish by Enrique Moya|last=Horowitz|first=Nathan D.|date=January 31, 2011|accessdate=24 November 2012}}</ref> He has published work in diverse literary genres in newspapers and magazines of [[Latin America]], the [[United States]] and [[Europe]]. He is the director of the Latin American - Austrian Literature Forum and of the Latin American Poetry Festival in [[Vienna]].
