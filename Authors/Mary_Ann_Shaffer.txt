==Biography==

Mary Ann Fiery was born on December 13, 1934 in [[Martinsburg, West Virginia]].<ref>[http://obit-obits.com/d20080301zp1 Obituary]</ref> She had an older sister, Cynthia. They were raised in nearby [[Romney, West Virginia]], but moved back to Martinsburg and went to high school there. Mary Ann was an alumna of [[Miami University]] in [[Oxford, Ohio]]. She married Carl Richard Shaffer in 1956, and in 1958 they moved to California, where they raised two daughters, Morgan and Liz. She worked in the public libraries of [[San Anselmo, California|San Anselmo]], [[Larkspur, California|Larkspur]],<ref name="Benson 2008" /> and [[San Rafael, California|San Rafael]] during her career. She also worked as an editor at [[Harper (publisher)|Harper & Row]], and as a bookseller at bookstores in Larkspur and Corte Madera.<ref name="Benson 2008" />

She died at her home in [[San Anselmo, California|San Anselmo]] on February 16, 2008.
