'''György Schwajda''' (24 March 1943 in [[Kispest]] – 19 April 2010 in [[Kaposvar]]) was a [[Hungary|Hungarian]] [[drama]]tist and [[theater director]]. Schwajda wrote several dramas and was the theater director of the city theater in Kaposvar.

He died after a long serious illness in Kaposvar, Hungary.
