'''Bruce Smith''' (born 1946) is an American [[poet]].

==Life==
Smith was born and raised in [[Philadelphia]]. He taught at the [[University of Alabama]], [[Phillips Academy|Phillips Academy, Andover]]<ref>{{Cite web|title=Poet Bruce Smith's Reading: Introduction by Katherine V. Halsey|url=https://www.thacher.org/page/news-detail?pk=754232|date=2012-01-18|website=The Thacher School|language=en|access-date=2020-05-21}}</ref> and now teaches at [[Syracuse University]].<ref>{{cite web |url=http://english.syr.edu/cwp/smith.htm |title=Archived copy |accessdate=2009-09-22 |url-status=dead |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20090614212034/http://english.syr.edu/cwp/smith.htm |archivedate=2009-06-14 }}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=http://english.syr.edu/Faculty/2009/Smith.htm |title=Archived copy |accessdate=2009-12-07 |url-status=dead |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20100609210837/http://english.syr.edu/Faculty/2009/Smith.htm |archivedate=2010-06-09 }}</ref> He has been a co-editor of the ''[[Graham House Review]]'' and a contributing editor of ''Born Magazine''.<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.pshares.org/authors/authorDetails.cfm?prmAuthorID=1427|title=Read By Author - Ploughshares|publisher=|accessdate=9 September 2016}}</ref>
