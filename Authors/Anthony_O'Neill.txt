'''Anthony O'Neill,''' born in Melbourne Australia in 1964, is a writer of fiction.<ref>{{cite news|url=http://www.theage.com.au/articles/2003/03/26/1048653742764.html|title=Murder in the city of voices|last=Fraser|first=Morag|date=May 29, 2003|work=[[The Age]]|accessdate=3 July 2011}}</ref> 

==Early life==
O'Neill's father was a policeman<ref name="encyclopedia.com">{{cite web|url=https://www.encyclopedia.com/arts/educational-magazines/oneill-anthony-1964|title=O'Neill, Anthony 1964- - Encyclopedia.com|website=www.encyclopedia.com|accessdate=18 April 2019}}</ref> and his mother, from whom he inherited a 'rich strain of Scottish blood',<ref name="auto">{{cite web |url= https://www.scotsman.com/news/anthony-o-neill-author-of-the-lamplighter-1-921142 |title=Anthony O’Neill author of ‘The Lamplighter’ |author= |date=31 January 2004 |website=[[scotsman]] |accessdate=17 February 2019 }}</ref> was a stenographer.<ref name="encyclopedia.com"/> Educated at the Christian Brothers College in St Kilda, he went straight into the workforce after school and devoted himself to his passion, writing.<ref name="encyclopedia.com"/> O'Neill has described his journey to publication as  'unspeakably long'.<ref>{{cite web |url=https://www.writing.ie/interviews/on-the-virtue-of-perseverance-dr-jekyll-and-mr-seek-by-anthony-oneill/|title=On the Virtue of Perseverance’ |author=Anthony O’Neill |date=31 August 2017 |website=[[writing.ie]] |accessdate=17 February 2019 }}</ref> 

==Writing and film career==
In October 2014, [[20th Century Fox]] preemptively purchased the film rights to his unpublished science fiction novel ''The Dark Side''.  The book was acquired for publication by [[Simon & Schuster]] at the [[Frankfurt Book Fair]], and was published in 2016.<ref>{{cite web |url=http://www.hollywoodreporter.com/heat-vision/fox-buys-hot-sci-fi-738448 |title=Fox Buys Hot Sci-Fi Book 'The Dark Side' for Steve Zaillian to Produce (Exclusive) |last=Siegel |first=Tatiana |date=6 October 2014 |work=[[The Hollywood Reporter]] |accessdate=7 October 2014 }}</ref><ref>{{cite web |url=https://uk.movies.yahoo.com/fox-wins-hot-frankfurt-book-195832345.html |title=Fox Wins Hot Frankfurt Book ‘The Dark Side’ For Film Rites To Produce |author=Mike Fleming Jr. |date=6 October 2014 |website=[[Yahoo! Movies]] |accessdate=7 October 2014 }}</ref><ref>{{cite web |url=http://bloody-disgusting.com/news/3316154/dark-side-moon-littered-criminals/ |title=‘The Dark Side’ of the Moon Littered with Criminals |author=MrDisgusting |date=6 October 2014 |website=[[Bloody Disgusting]] |accessdate=7 October 2014 }}</ref><ref>{{cite web |url=https://www.writing.ie/interviews/on-the-virtue-of-perseverance-dr-jekyll-and-mr-seek-by-anthony-oneill/|title=On the Virtue of Perseverance |author=Anthony O’Neill |date=31 August 2017 |website=[[writing.ie]] |accessdate=17 February 2019 }}</ref> 

==Personal life==
O'Neill has lived now for a number of years  in Edinburgh which he has called 'a deliriously atmospheric city - for me the most stimulating in the world.'<ref name="auto"/>
