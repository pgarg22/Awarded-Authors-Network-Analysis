'''Ljubivoje Ršumović''' ([[Serbian Cyrillic alphabet|Serbian Cyrillic]]: Љубивоје Ршумовић; born 3 July 1939) is a Serbian poet and writer.<ref name="Politika ref">{{cite web |url=http://www.politika.rs/scc/clanak/384217/Drustvo/Penzijom-pomazem-sinovima |author=''[[Politika]]'' |title=Penzijom pomažem sinovima |access-date=2019-09-30 |date=2017-07-03 |language=sr}}</ref>

Ršumović is predominantly a writer and poet for children; however, a portion of his work is literature for adults.

==Biography==
He was born on 3 July 1939 in the village of [[Ljubiš]] in the [[Zlatibor]] Mountains. His parents were Mihailo and Milesa Ršumović. He was educated in Ljubiš, [[Čajetina]], [[Užice]], and [[Belgrade]]. He has a bachelor's degree from the [[University of Belgrade]]'s [[University of Belgrade Faculty of Philology|Faculty of Philology]].<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.poezijanoci.com/decije-pesme/ljubivoje-rsumovic-pesme-za-decu.html|title=Ljubivoje Ršumović - pesme za decu {{!}} Pesmice za decu|website=www.poezijanoci.com|access-date=2019-12-18}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.lektire.rs/pisac/ljubivoje-rsumovic/|title=Ljubivoje Ršumović {{!}} Lektire.rs|website=www.lektire.rs|access-date=2019-12-18}}</ref>
