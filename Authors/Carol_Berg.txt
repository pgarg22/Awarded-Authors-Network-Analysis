'''Carol Berg''' (born 1948)<ref>{{LCAuth|no00068170|Carol Berg|13|}}</ref> is the author of [[fantasy fiction|fantasy]] novels, including the books from the ''Rai-Kirah'' series, ''Song of the Beast'', the books from ''The Bridge of D'Arnath'' series, the ''Lighthouse'' novels, and ''Collegia Magica''.<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.carolberg.com/bibliography.html|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20190829152134/https://www.carolberg.com/bibliography.html|url-status=dead|archive-date=2019-08-29|title=Carol Berg Bibliography|website=www.carolberg.com|access-date=2019-08-29}}</ref> She also writes the Chimera series under the pen name '''Cate Glass'''.<ref>{{Cite web|url=http://carolberg.com/index.html|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20190601000612/http://carolberg.com/index.html|url-status=dead|archive-date=2019-06-01|title=Cate Glass: Author|website=www.categlass.com|access-date=2019-08-29}}</ref>

Berg holds a degree in mathematics from [[Rice University]], and a degree in computer science from the [[University of Colorado system|University of Colorado]].<ref>[http://errol.oclc.org/laf/no00-68170.html Library of Congress name authority file]</ref> Before writing full-time, she designed software. She lives in [[Colorado]], and is the mother of three boys.
