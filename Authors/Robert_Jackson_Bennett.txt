'''Robert Jackson Bennett''' (born 1984) is an American writer of [[speculative fiction]].<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.sfsignal.com/archives/2012/02/exclusive-interview-robert-jackson-bennett/ |title=Exclusive Interview: Robert Jackson Bennett Says 'The Troupe' is About How You Perceive Yourself and the World |publisher=SF Signal |date=2012-02-13 |accessdate=2013-09-19}}</ref>

==Biography==
Bennett was born in [[Baton Rouge]], Louisiana.<ref>[http://www.phantastik-couch.de/robert-jackson-bennett.html  Phantastik-couch (German)]</ref> He graduated from the [[University of Texas]] with special honors in English in 2005,<ref>[https://utdirect.utexas.edu/apps/degree/degrees/nlogon/?s_last_name_read=BENNETT%2C+ROSEMARY&s_last_isn_read=729932&s_first_name_read=BENNETT%2C+ROBERT&s_first_isn_read=101910&s_first_time_sw=X&s_start_name=Bennett%2C+Robert+Jackson&s_start_page=Submit University of Texas at Austin: Degrees and Dates of Attendance]</ref> and settled down in [[Austin, Texas]].<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.austinchronicle.com/arts/2013-02-15/america-with-tentacles/ |title=America, With Tentacles: Speculative fiction writer Robert Jackson Bennett travels back to the future |publisher=Austin Chronicle |date=2013-02-15 |accessdate=2018-09-12}}</ref>

His debut as an author was the novel ''[[Mr. Shivers]]'' (2010). He went on to write ''[[The Company Man (book)|The Company Man]]'' (2011), ''[[The Troupe]]'' (2012), and ''[[American Elsewhere]]'' (2013).<ref name=FantasyLiterature>{{cite web|url=http://www.fantasyliterature.com/author-interviews/robert-jackson-bennett/ |title=Marion chats with Robert Jackson Bennett &#124; Fantasy Literature: Fantasy and Science Fiction Book and Audiobook Reviews |publisher=Fantasy Literature |accessdate=2013-09-19}}</ref>

Bennett's fifth novel, ''[[City of Stairs]]'' (2014), was the first of a fantasy trilogy, ''[[The Divine Cities]]'', followed by ''City of Blades'' (2016) and ''City of Miracles'' (2017).  ''The Divine Cities'' was nominated for Best Series by the [[Hugo Award|Hugo Awards]] in 2018, but lost to ''World of the Five Gods'' by [[Lois McMaster Bujold]].<ref>{{cite web |url=http://www.thehugoawards.org/2018/08/2018-hugo-award-winners/ |title=2018 Hugo Award Winners |date=2018-08-19 |access-date=2018-09-12}}</ref>

In August 2018, Bennett published ''[[Foundryside]]'', the first installment of his new series, ''The Founders Trilogy''.<ref>{{cite web |url=http://robertjacksonbennett.com/books/foundryside |title=Robert Jackson Bennett - Foundryside |access-date=2018-09-12}}</ref>

==Works==
*''[[Mr. Shivers]]'' (2010; [[Orbit Books|Orbit]]). Won the [[Shirley Jackson Award]] and the [[British Fantasy Awards#2011|British Fantasy Awards' Sydney J Bounds Newcomer Award]].
*''[[The Company Man (novel)|The Company Man]]'' (2011; Orbit). Won the Special Citation of the [[Philip K. Dick Award]] and an [[Edgar Award#2012 winners|Edgar Award]] for Best Paperback Original.
*''The Troupe'' (2012; Orbit). 
* ''American Elsewhere'' (2013; Orbit). Won the Shirley Jackson Award for best novel.
*''Vigilance'' (2019; Tor.com)
