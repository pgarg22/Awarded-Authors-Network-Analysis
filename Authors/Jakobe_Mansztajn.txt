'''Jakobe Mansztajn''' (born 1982, in [[Gdańsk]]) is a Polish [[poet]] and [[blog]]ger.

==Life and career==

He studied [[psychology]] at the [[University of Gdańsk]]. He works as deputy [[editor]] of the literary quarterly ''Korespondencja z ojcem''. He is the author of the poetry collection ''Vienna High Life'' (2009) for which he has received many awards, including the prestigious 2010 Wroclaw [[Silesius Poetry Award]] in the Best Debut category,<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.culture.pl/pl/culture/artykuly/wy_in_nagroda_silesius_2010 |title=Wrocławska Nagroda Poetycka Silesius 2010 |publisher=www.culture.pl (Instytut Adama Mickiewicza) |language=Polish |access-date=13 April 2011 |url-status=dead |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20101115183038/http://www.culture.pl/pl/culture/artykuly/wy_in_nagroda_silesius_2010 |archive-date=15 November 2010 |df= }}</ref> the 2010 Sztorm Roku Award founded by ''[[Gazeta Wyborcza]]'',<ref>{{cite web|url=http://trojmiasto.gazeta.pl/trojmiasto/1,35636,9125200,Sztormy_Roku_2010_rozdane___laureaci_oraz_zdjecia.html|title=Sztormy Roku 2010|publisher=www.gazeta.pl (Gazeta Wyborcza)|language=Polish|accessdate=13 April 2011}}</ref> and has also been nominated for the [[Gdynia Literary Prize]].<ref>{{cite web|url=http://kulturaonline.pl/nagroda,literacka,gdynia,nominacje,tytul,artykul,8924.html|title=Nagroda Literacka Gdynia 2010|publisher=www.kulturaonline.pl (Kultura Online)|language=Polish|accessdate=13 April 2011}}</ref> He was one of the initiators of the ''Zjednoczenie czytelnicze'' social campaign aimed at promoting reading books in Poland. His debut collection has been described as "one of the most distinctive in the past years in [[Polish literature]]" and gained wide critical acclaim. His works have been translated into many languages including German, Hebrew, English,<ref>{{cite web|url=http://off-press.org/main/poetry/jakobe-mansztajn/|title=OFF_Press publications|publisher=www.off-press.org (OFF_Press Publishing House)|accessdate=13 April 2011}}</ref> Belarusian, and Norwegian.

He is a co-host of the satirical [[Internet TV]] programme ''Make Poland Great Again''.<ref>{{cite news|url=https://mpga.gazeta.pl/mpga/0,0.html |title=Make Poland Great Again |access-date=2019-05-06}}</ref>
