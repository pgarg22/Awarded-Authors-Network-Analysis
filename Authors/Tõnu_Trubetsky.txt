'''Tõnu Trubetsky''' (born 24 April 1963), also known as '''Tony Blackplait''', is an [[Estonians|Estonian]] [[punk rock]]/[[glam punk]] [[musician]], [[film]] and [[music video]] [[film director|director]], and [[individualist anarchist]].

==Political activity==

Trubetsky is a former member of the [[Estonian Greens]]. He stood as a candidate in the [[2007 Estonian parliamentary election|Estonian parliamentary election]] in 2007<ref>{{cite news|url=http://www.postimees.ee/130107/esileht/olulised_teemad/riigikogu_2007/eestimaa_rohelised/238999.php|title=Tõnu Trubetsky pürib roheliste ridades riigikokku|publisher=[[Postimees]]|language=Estonian|access-date=10 January 2010|archive-url=https://archive.today/20120917212519/http://wwx.postimees.ee/130107/esileht/olulised_teemad/riigikogu_2007/eestimaa_rohelised/238999.php |archive-date=17 September 2012 |url-status=dead}}</ref> and the [[2009 European Parliament election in Estonia|European Parliament election]] in 2009.<ref>{{cite news|url=http://uudised.err.ee/index.php?06157644|title=Tõnu Trubetsky kandideerib europarlamenti|publisher=[[Eesti Rahvusringhääling]] |language=Estonian|accessdate=10 January 2010}}</ref>  On 5 August 2010, Trubetsky announced he had left the Greens for the [[Estonian Centre Party|Centre Party]].<ref name="delfi_2010-08-05">{{cite news |title=Tõnu Trubetsky astus Keskerakonda |url=http://www.delfi.ee/news/paevauudised/eesti/tonu-trubetsky-astus-keskerakonda.d?id=32464289 |publisher=[[Delfi (web portal)|Delfi]] |date=August 5, 2010 |accessdate=August 5, 2010 |language=Estonian |trans-title=Tõnu Trubetsky joined the Centre Party }}</ref>
