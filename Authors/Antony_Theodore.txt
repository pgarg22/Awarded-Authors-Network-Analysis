'''Antony Theodore''' (born 1954) is a bilingual German poet, pastor and educator who writes in both English and German.<ref>{{cite web|url= https://www.ruhrnachrichten.de/nachrichten/pastor-antony-theodore-liest-aus-seinem-vierten-buch-170947.html |title= Pastor Antony Theodore liest aus seinem vierten Buch |access-date=July 8, 2021|language=de}}</ref> He writes mostly on spiritual teachings of [[Jesus Christ]] and underlying unity of all world religions.<ref>{{cite web|url= http://creationandcriticism.com/five_poems_of_antony_theodore.html |title= Creation and Criticism – Poems of Antony Theodore |access-date=July 8, 2021}}</ref><ref>{{cite book|last = Mangattu| first = M. | url= https://www.researchgate.net/publication/320264065 |title= Pain Pleasure and Paradox in Poetry |pages = 20–21 | access-date=July 8, 2021}}</ref> His spiritual poems are read as sermons at [[United Church of Christ]].<ref>{{cite web|url= https://www.iaoucc.org/sermons/12.13.20.pdf |title= Virtual Liturgy – Poem "Crying out in Wilderness" by Antony Theodore |access-date=July 8, 2021}}</ref> He has also written spiritual biography of the American mystic [[Thomas Merton]].<ref>{{cite book| first =Antony| last=Theodore| url= https://www.worldcat.org/title/thomas-mertons-mystical-quest-for-the-union-with-god/oclc/30320691 | date= January 1, 1993 | access-date = July 6, 2021 | title = Thomas Merton's Mystical Quest for Union with God | pages= 88–92 | oclc=30320691}}</ref> More than 5000 of his poems have been posted on various poetry websites like Poem Hunter, where he is consistently ranked among top world poets.<ref>{{cite web|url= https://www.poemhunter.com/poets/|title= Top 500 World Poets |access-date=June 12, 2021|publisher=Poem Hunter}}</ref>

==Life and works==
Antony Theodore was born on November 9, 1954, as John Antony Theodore Vazhakuttathil in [[Alleppey]] district of [[Kerala]], India.<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.ventura-verlag.de/autorinnen/pastor-antony/ |title=Antony Theodore - Author Information |access-date=June 13, 2021 |publisher=Ventura Verlag}}</ref> After early demise of his foster mother Genova Maa, Antony migrated to Germany. He studied at [[Fordham University]] in [[New York City]] and [[Ludwig Maximilian University of Munich|Ludwig Maximilian University]] in [[Munich]]. He obtained two bachelor's degrees and also two doctorate degrees in English literature and philosophy of world religions.<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.kohinoorbooks.com/Antony/ |title=Antony Theodore - Life & Works}}</ref>
In 1986 when Antony was travelling from [[Mumbai]] to [[Frankfurt]] the [[Pan Am|Pan Am 73]] flight carrying him got hijacked at [[Karachi]] airport.<ref>{{cite news|url= https://www.bbc.com/news/world-asia-35800683 |title= Inside a Hijacking – Inside Story of Pan Am 73 |first=Megha |last =Mohan |publisher = BBC|date=March 31, 2016|access-date=June 12, 2021}}</ref> In the 16 hour siege 22 of the 400 passengers were killed.<ref>{{cite web|url= https://www.justice.gov/usao-dc/victim-witness-assistance/hijacking-pan-am-flight-73 |title= Hijacking of Pan Am Flight #73 |date= 19 February 2015 |publisher = United States Department of Justice|access-date=June 12, 2021}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url= https://www.indiatoday.in/magazine/cover-story/story/19860930-pan-am-hijack-in-karachi-pakistan-security-forces-handling-of-situation-raises-questions-801277-1986-09-30 |title= Pan Am Hijack in Karachi |publisher = India Today|first=Zahid|last=Hussain| date= September 30, 1986| access-date=July 8, 2021}}</ref> Antony miraculously escaped with four gunshot wounds.<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.casemine.com/judgement/us/5914bd09add7b0493479ff32 |title= Hijacking of Pan Am Airlines - Case Details & Court Order |access-date=June 12, 2021}}</ref> After this traumatic experience Antony devoted his life to education and social service. He was ordained a pastor and worked as a chaplain in St Martin, Munich.<ref>{{cite web|url= https://www.ventura-verlag.de/autorinnen/pastor-antony/ |title= Pastor Antony - Uberschrift|publisher=Ventura Verlag|access-date=July 8, 2021|language=de}}</ref> He has lived and worked most of his life in [[Dortmund]] in Germany<ref>{{cite web|url= https://www.pv-am-phoenixsee.de/menschen/pastoral-team-2/  |title= Pastoral Team Dortmund – Dr Antony Theodore Vazhakuttathil|access-date=July 8, 2021}}</ref> although he has retained his association with [[Diocese of Alleppey]].<ref>{{cite web|url=  http://www.dioceseofalleppey.com/vg/diocesan.php?cmd=priests |title= Diocese of Alleppey – Rev. Fr. Antony Theodore, PhD |access-date=July 8, 2021}}</ref>  He often conducts charitable fund raising activities for health and social work.<ref>{{cite web|url= http://support.mshf.on.ca/site/TR/Events/TeamRaiser-Events?team_id=1260&pg=team&fr_id=1310 |title= Help Combat Covid-19 – Antony Theodore & Friends |access-date=July 8, 2021}}</ref>
More than 5000 of Antony's poems have been published on poetry websites like Poet Freak, Poem Hunter, Starlite Cafe, Micropoet and Poet's Garden etc. Besides the teachings of Christ, he has also written extensively on the universal truths expressed in the holy books of other world religions like [[Hinduism]], [[Buddhism]], [[Islam]], [[Judaism]], [[Confucianism]] and [[Zoroastrianism]] etc.<ref>{{cite book|url=https://books.google.com/books?id=-cwcEAAAQBAJ |title= Book "Jesus Christ in Love" |pages=245–251|isbn= 9788194283539 |access-date=2021-07-08|last1= Theodore |first1= Antony |date= December 2019 }}</ref> His poem anti-abortion poem "''I am Your Baby, Mother''" has been translated into more than twenty languages including French, Italian, Russian, Greek, Arabic and Hindi.<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.poemhunter.com/poem/i-am-your-baby-mum/  |title=I Am Your Baby Mum by Antony Theodore |date=26 August 2015 |access-date=July 7, 2021|publisher=Poem Hunter}}</ref>
