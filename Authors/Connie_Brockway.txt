'''Connie Brockway''' (born December 16, 1954) is a [[best-seller|best-selling]] [[United States|American]] [[author]] of over twenty [[historical romance|historical]] and contemporary [[romance novels]] since 1994.

==Biography==
Connie Brockway was born on December 16, 1954, in [[Minneapolis]], [[Minnesota]], but spent several years in suburban [[Buffalo, New York|Buffalo]], [[New York (state)|New York]]. Her family returned to [[Edina, Minnesota]] where she attended high school. In 1976, Brockway received a [[Bachelor of Arts|B.A.]] from [[Macalester College]] in [[St. Paul, Minnesota]]. While earning an [[Master's degree|M.A.]] in [[Creative Writing]] at the [[University of Minnesota]], she met her future husband, David Brockway, medical student, now a family physician.<ref name="conniebrockway.com">{{Citation|title=Connie Brockway's Bio|url=http://www.conniebrockway.com/about.php|date=2012-06-25}}</ref> They married in November 1976.<ref name="highbeam.com">{{Citation|title=Connie Brockway at highbeam.com|url=http://www.highbeam.com/doc/1G2-3415700023.html|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20150402194319/http://www.highbeam.com/doc/1G2-3415700023.html|url-status=dead|archive-date=2015-04-02|date=2012-06-25}}</ref> They have a daughter. The couple currently live in Minnesota.

Brockway decided to take a year and try to write a book once her daughter entered school. In 1994, Brockway published her first book, ''Promise Me Heaven''. Over 1.5 million copies of her books are now in print, and they can be found in thirteen countries. Brockway has been a finalist for the [[Romance Writers of America]]'s [[RITA Award]] eight times, winning twice. Although Brockway made her mark with historical romance, in 2005 she announced that she would take a hiatus from historical romance and begin writing contemporary romance novels.<ref>[http://www.likesbooks.com/conniebrockway2005.html All About Romance Novels - 2005 interview with Connie Brockway<!-- Bot generated title -->] {{webarchive|url=https://web.archive.org/web/20061228045338/http://www.likesbooks.com/conniebrockway2005.html |date=2006-12-28 }}</ref>
