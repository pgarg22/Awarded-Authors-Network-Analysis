'''Jeffrey Daniels''' is a [[Chicago]]-raised [[African-American]] [[poet]], artist, and professor at [[Harold Washington College]]. He has previously taught at [[Columbia College Chicago]] and the [[Art Institute of Chicago|School of the Art Institute of Chicago]].

*''Black Girls and Bicycles'' (2006, {{ISBN|0-9760508-1-1}})
