'''Laura Frankos-Turtledove''' is an American author born February 9, 1960. She writes as '''Laura Frankos'''.

==Biography==
Frankos is married to the science fiction author [[Harry Turtledove]] and has three daughters: Alison, Rachel, and Rebecca. Her brother is fantasy author [[Steven Frankos]].<ref name="frankos" />

==Literary career==
Frankos has written [[Mystery fiction|mysteries]], [[fantasy]] and [[science fiction]] stories. She has published one mystery novel, ''Saint Oswald's Niche'', which is currently out of print, and ''The Broadway Musical Quiz Book''.<ref>{{cite web|title=Fiction Book Review: St. Oswald's Niche by Laura Frankos|url=http://www.publishersweekly.com/978-0-8041-0530-9|website=PublishersWeekly.com}}</ref><ref name="frankos">{{Cite web | last = Silver | first = Steven H | authorlink = Steven H Silver | title = Associated Authors | work = Harry Turtledove Web Site | publisher = SF Site | year = 2011 | url = http://www.stevenhsilver.com/associational.html | access-date = April 5, 2011}}</ref> Frankos also writes the Broadway history column "The Great White Wayback Machine" and has published numerous trivia quizzes about Broadway plays.<ref>{{Cite book | last = Frankos | first = Laura | title = The Broadway Musical Quiz Book | publisher = Applause Theatre & Cinema Books | date = October 2010 | location = Milwaukee, WI | page = Back Cover | isbn = 978-1-4234-9275-7}}</ref>
