'''Deb Marlowe''' is an [[United States|American]] [[author]] for [[historical romance|historical]] [[romance novels]].  She is a 2007 winner of the [[Romance Writers of America]]'s Golden Heart Award, granted to unpublished authors.<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.rwanational.org/cs/contests_and_awards/2007_rita_and_golden_heart_award_winners_announcement|title=2007 RITA and Golden Heart Award Winners|publisher=[[Romance Writers of America]]|date=July 2007|accessdate=2007-09-09|url-status=dead|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20070811230212/http://rwanational.org/cs/contests_and_awards/2007_rita_and_golden_heart_award_winners_announcement|archivedate=2007-08-11|df=}}</ref>  Eleven days after entering her second manuscript, ''The Lost Jewel,'' for consideration for the Golden Heart Award, Marlowe received word that her first manuscript had been purchased by [[Mills and Boon]].  Following the competition, Mills and Boon also purchased ''The Lost Jewel.''<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.debmarlowe.com/index.aspx|title=Deb Marlowe|publisher=DebMarlowe.com|date=2007|accessdate=2007-09-09}} {{Dead link|date=October 2010|bot=H3llBot}}</ref>
