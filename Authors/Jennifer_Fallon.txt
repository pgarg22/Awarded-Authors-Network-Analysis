'''Jennifer Fallon''' (born 1959) is an Australian author of fantasy and science fiction. She is also a businesswoman, trainer and business consultant.

Jennifer has a master's degree from the Creative Arts faculty of [[Queensland University of Technology|QUT]]. A computer trainer and application specialist in her "day job", Jennifer currently works in the IT industry and spends a month each year working at Scott Base in Antarctica.

==Biography==
Jennifer Fallon was born in [[Melbourne, Australia]] and after living in Central Australia for a number of years, now resides in the South Island of [[Oxford, New Zealand|New Zealand]]. She has sold over 750,000 books world-wide, including three trilogies and one tetralogy.

She is published by Snapping Turtle Books worldwide, in addition to some titles through [[HarperCollins|Voyager Books]] in Australia, [[Tor Books|Tor]] and [[Random House]] in the United States, [[Orbit Books|Orbit]] in the United Kingdom, [[AST (publisher)|AST]] in Russia, [[Heyne Verlag|Heyne]] and [[Egmont Publishing|Egmont]] in Germany and [[Luitingh Fantasy]] in the Netherlands. She has also co-authored a tie-in novel, [[Stargate SG-1: Roswell]].
