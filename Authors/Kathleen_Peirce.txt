'''Kathleen Peirce''' (born 1956, [[Moline, Illinois]], United States) is an [[Americans|American]] [[poet]].<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.blackbird.vcu.edu/v2n2/poetry/peirce_k/index.htm|title=Kathleen Peirce, Blackbird|website=Blackbird.vcu.edu|accessdate=13 November 2018}}</ref> She graduated from the [[Iowa Writer's Workshop]] in 1988.  She currently teaches at [[Texas State University]] in San Marcos, Texas,<ref>{{Cite web |url=http://www.english.txstate.edu/people-contacts/faculty/peirce.html |title=Archived copy |access-date=2010-05-16 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20100621165148/http://www.english.txstate.edu/people-contacts/faculty/peirce.html |archive-date=2010-06-21 |url-status=dead }}</ref> for the [[Texas State University MFA]].<ref>{{cite web |url=http://www.mfatxstate.com/faculty.asp |title=Archived copy |accessdate=2010-05-16 |url-status=dead |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20100129115742/http://www.mfatxstate.com/faculty.asp |archivedate=2010-01-29 }}</ref> She has one son.
