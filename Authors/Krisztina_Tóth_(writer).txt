'''Krisztina Tóth''' (born 5 December 1967 in [[Budapest]]) is a [[Hungary|Hungarian]] writer, poet and translator.<ref>{{cite web |url=https://www.munzinger.de/search/go/document.jsp?id=00000030056 |title=Krisztina Tóth |language=German |publisher=[[Munzinger-Archiv]] |accessdate=2018-08-17 }}</ref><ref name="OzsváthTurner2014">{{cite book|author1=Zsuzsanna Ozsváth|author2=Frederick Turner|title=Light within the Shade: Eight Hundred Years of Hungarian Poetry|url=https://books.google.com/books?id=l23iAwAAQBAJ&pg=PA251|date=2 July 2014|publisher=Syracuse University Press|isbn=978-0-8156-5274-8|page=251}}</ref>

==Selected works==
* ''Vonalkód: tizenöt történet''. Budapest: [[Magvető|Magvető Kiadó]], 2006
* ''Pixel''. Budapest: Magvető Kiadó, 2011
* ''Akvárium''. Budapest: Magvető Kiadó, 2013<ref>{{cite web |url=https://www.hkw.de/en/programm/projekte/2015/internationaler_literaturpreis_2015/shortlist_2015/krisztina_toth_gyoergy_buda_aquarium.php |title=Krisztina Tóth: Aquarium |publisher=[[International Literature Award]] |accessdate=2018-08-17 }}</ref>
