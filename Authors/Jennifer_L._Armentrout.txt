'''Jennifer Lynn Armentrout''' (born June 11, 1980), also known by the pseudonym '''J. Lynn''',<ref name=":0" /> is an American writer of contemporary romance, new adult and fantasy.<ref name=wc>{{Cite web |url=http://www.worldcat.org/wcidentities/lccn-no2011131139 |title=WorldCat identities |access-date=2014-04-27 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20140427135149/http://www.worldcat.org/wcidentities/lccn-no2011131139 |archive-date=2014-04-27 |url-status=live }}</ref><ref>{{cite news |last=Mauck |first=Kaitlyn |title=Local author continues success with latest releases |newspaper=[[The Journal (West Virginia newspaper)|The Journal]] |date=January 23, 2014 |url=http://www.journal-news.net/page/content.detail/id/603823/Local-author-continues-success-with-latest-releases.html?nav=5094 |access-date=2014-02-07 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20140222223852/http://www.journal-news.net/page/content.detail/id/603823/Local-author-continues-success-with-latest-releases.html?nav=5094 |archive-date=2014-02-22 |url-status=dead }}</ref> Several of her works have made ''[[The New York Times]]'' Best Seller list.<ref>{{Cite web |url=http://www.jenniferarmentrout.com/a-special-thank-you/ |title=A SPECIAL THANK YOU |date=August 13, 2014 |access-date=2014-08-28 |website=JENNIFER L. ARMENTROUT |last=Armentrout |first=Jennifer |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20140818015051/http://www.jenniferarmentrout.com/a-special-thank-you/ |archive-date=2014-08-18 |url-status=dead }}</ref><ref>{{Cite web |url=https://www.nytimes.com/best-sellers-books/2014-03-16/young-adult/list.html |title=Best Sellers |date=March 16, 2014 |access-date=2014-08-28 |website=[[The New York Times]] |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20141017175803/http://www.nytimes.com/best-sellers-books/2014-03-16/young-adult/list.html |archive-date=2014-10-17 |url-status=dead}}</ref>

== Early life ==
Jennifer L. Armentrout was born on June 11<ref name=":0" /> in West Virginia.<ref name=":1" /> She was inspired to become a writer after reading the works of [[L. J. Smith (author)|L.J. Smith]], such as [[The Vampire Diaries]], [[The Secret Circle|The Secret Circle Series]], The Forbidden Games Series, and a myriad of others.<ref name=":0" /> The book series that remained close to her heart was The Forbidden Games, with the final novel bringing her to tears.<ref name=":0" /> Upon completing the series she decided she wanted to leave the same impact on her future readers.<ref name=":0" />

Her first experience with writing an actual novel was in high school during algebra class.<ref name=":0" /><ref name=":3">{{Cite web|title=Interview with a Barefoot Writer: Jennifer L. Armentrout|url=https://www.awai.com/2018/03/interview-with-jennifer-l-armentrout/|access-date=2021-05-28|website=www.awai.com|archive-date=May 7, 2021|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20210507122514/https://www.awai.com/2018/03/interview-with-jennifer-l-armentrout/|url-status=live}}</ref> Despite her desire to be an author, she went to college and graduated with a major in psychology.<ref name=":3" />

== Career ==
Despite receiving a great deal of rejections before her career began, Armentrout's first book was published in 2011.<ref name=":0" /> As of September 2019, she had fifty-three of her fifty-seven total written works published.<ref name=":4" /> The majority of her published works for young adults are romance, fantasy and paranormal, and some contemporary and science fiction.<ref name=":1" /> With her pseudonym J. Lynn,<ref name=":0" /> she writes suspenseful romance novels for her adult readers.<ref name=":3" />

In 2013, her [[Young adult novel|young adult]] novel ''Obsidian'' has been optioned by [[Sierra Pictures]],<ref>{{cite news|last=McClintock|first=Pamela|date=July 22, 2013|title=Sierra Pictures Acquires 'Obsidian' for the Big Screen (Exclusive)|newspaper=The Hollywood Reporter|url=https://www.hollywoodreporter.com/news/sierra-pictures-acquires-obsidian-big-590261|url-status=live|access-date=2013-10-22|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20131027031521/http://www.hollywoodreporter.com/news/sierra-pictures-acquires-obsidian-big-590261|archive-date=2013-10-27}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|last=Rosenfield|first=Kat|date=July 23, 2013|title=YA Blockbuster 'Obsidian' Is Off to the Movies|url=http://hollywoodcrush.mtv.com/2013/07/23/obsidian-jennifer-armentrout/|url-status=dead|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20131022054838/http://hollywoodcrush.mtv.com/2013/07/23/obsidian-jennifer-armentrout/|archive-date=2013-10-22|access-date=2013-10-22|work=MTV: Hollywood Crush: Movie News|publisher=Viacom International Inc.}}</ref> but the rights were later returned to the author.<ref>{{Cite web|date=2019-10-04|title=FAQ|url=https://jenniferlarmentrout.com/faq/|access-date=2021-10-10|website=Jennifer L. Armentrout|language=en-US}}</ref> In 2015,  Armentrout's colleague suggested that she do a book signing for the first release in her ''Titan'' series.<ref name=":0" /> She was opposed to doing a lonely and awkward book signing, so she asked authors to come join her in the daylong event.<ref name=":0" /><ref name=":5">{{Cite web|last=Drakeford|first=Cortney|date=2018-03-23|title=Matt Lauer Update: Anchor Isn’t Losing Famous Friends Over Sexual Harassment Allegations|url=https://www.ibtimes.com/matt-lauer-update-anchor-isnt-losing-famous-friends-over-sexual-harassment-2665633|access-date=2021-05-28|website=International Business Times|archive-date=May 14, 2021|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20210514140323/https://www.ibtimes.com/matt-lauer-update-anchor-isnt-losing-famous-friends-over-sexual-harassment-2665633|url-status=live}}</ref> After this, Armentrout created ApollyCon, which rapidly became a new way for authors and readers to come together in celebration of recently released books.<ref name=":0" /><ref name=":1" /><ref name=":5" /> The convention's success continues to grow with each event.<ref name=":5" />

Throughout her career, Armentrout continues to reach success.<ref name=":1" /> She is a #1 New York Times<ref name=":0" /><ref name=":1" /><ref name=":3" /> and a USA Today Best Seller.<ref name=":0" /><ref name=":3" /> She has published books independently and with publishing companies, earning the title of being a "hybrid" author.<ref name=":3" />

She likes writing stories for both age groups to prevent writer burnout.<ref name=":1" /> Armentrout is highly prolific and states that she writes for eight hours a day almost every day.
