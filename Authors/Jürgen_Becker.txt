'''Jürgen Becker''' (born August 27, 1959) is a [[Germany|German]] [[comedian]], [[kabarett]] artist, and actor.

== Life ==
Becker was born and attended school in [[Cologne]], [[Germany]]. He became a [[graphic designer]] in the German company [[4711 (brand)|4711]]. Later Becker studied [[social science]] in Cologne.

In 1983, Becker was a founding member of ''[[Stunksitzung]]'' in Cologne, and he was its president from 1984 to 1995. Since 1992 Becker has performed in the television show [[Mitternachtsspitzen]].
