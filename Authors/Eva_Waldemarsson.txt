'''Eva Sylvia Waldemarsson''' (November 16, 1903 &ndash; May 24, 1986) was a [[Sweden|Swedish]] writer, mainly known for her [[historical novel]]s.<ref name=alex>{{cite web |url=https://www.alex.se/lexicon/article/waldemarsson-eva |title=Waldemarsson, Eva |website=alex.se |language=sv}}</ref>

The daughter of a wholesaler in [[Rinkaby]], she taught for several years after completing her education. She later worked as an office assistant in [[Kristianstad]].<ref name=nordic>{{cite web |url=https://nordicwomensliterature.net/writers/waldemarsson-eva-sylvia/ |title=Eva Sylvia Waldemarsson |work=History of Nordic Women's Literature}}</ref><ref name=alex/>

Her first novel ''Himlavargen'', published in 1959, was named best novel in [[Scania]] province. Her novels are written in local speech.<ref name=nordic/>

== Selected works<ref name=nordic/> ==
* ''Himlaland'', novel (1961)
* ''Madonneleken'', novel (1967)
* ''Stadsrådinnan'', novel (1982)
* ''Min far sköt en stork'', novel (1984)
