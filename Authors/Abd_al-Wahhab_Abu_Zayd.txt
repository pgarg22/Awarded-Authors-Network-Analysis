'''Abd al-Wahhab Khalil Abu Zayd''' ([[Arabic]]: [[:ar:عبد الوهاب أبو زيد|عبد الوهاب أبو زيد]]) is a Saudi [[Translation|translator]] and [[poet]].<ref>{{Cite web|date=2020-04-19|title=عبد الوهاب أبو زيد {{!}} الشرق الأوسط|url=https://web.archive.org/web/20200419190721/https://aawsat.com/home/writer/Abdalwahab%20Abuziad|access-date=2021-04-12|website=web.archive.org}}</ref> He publishes his poetic, literary, and translational works in Saudi newspapers.<ref>{{Cite web|date=2020-04-19|title=عبد الوهاب أبو زيد: اخترتُ شعر الحب حين أصبحت الرومانسية تهمة {{!}} الشرق الأوسط|url=https://web.archive.org/web/20200419190723/https://aawsat.com/home/article/1000176/%D8%B9%D8%A8%D8%AF-%D8%A7%D9%84%D9%88%D9%87%D8%A7%D8%A8-%D8%A3%D8%A8%D9%88-%D8%B2%D9%8A%D8%AF-%D8%A7%D8%AE%D8%AA%D8%B1%D8%AA%D9%8F-%D8%B4%D8%B9%D8%B1-%D8%A7%D9%84%D8%AD%D8%A8-%D8%AD%D9%8A%D9%86-%D8%A3%D8%B5%D8%A8%D8%AD%D8%AA-%D8%A7%D9%84%D8%B1%D9%88%D9%85%D8%A7%D9%86%D8%B3%D9%8A%D8%A9-%D8%AA%D9%87%D9%85%D8%A9|access-date=2021-04-12|website=web.archive.org}}</ref> Among his best-known translations are works by the American singer and poet [[Bob Dylan]].<ref>{{Cite web|date=2020-04-19|title=سيرة بوب ديلان في ترجمة عربية|url=https://web.archive.org/web/20200419190726/https://alqabas.com/article/482183-%D8%B3%D9%8A%D8%B1%D8%A9-%D8%A8%D9%88%D8%A8-%D8%AF%D9%8A%D9%84%D8%A7%D9%86-%D9%81%D9%8A-%D8%AA%D8%B1%D8%AC%D9%85%D8%A9-%D8%B9%D8%B1%D8%A8%D9%8A%D8%A9|access-date=2021-04-12|website=web.archive.org}}</ref>

== Publications ==

=== Books ===

* Li Ma Asha’a (I get what I want), a collection of poetry published by Al-Sharqiyyah Literary Club in 2008.<ref>{{Cite web|date=2020-04-19|title=عبد الوهاب أبو زيد: كثير من المثقفين رسموا صورا زائفة عن ذواتهم|url=https://web.archive.org/web/20200419190729/https://www.addustour.com/articles/454476?s=a1fe7a801d08e5d753bc534b82406766|access-date=2021-04-12|website=web.archive.org}}</ref>
* Wala Qablaha Min Nesa'ain Wala Ba'adaha Min Ahad (No women exist neither before her nor after her) published by Dar Tawa in 2013.<ref>{{Cite web|date=2020-04-19|title=جريدة الرياض {{!}} عبد الوهاب أبو زيد يستعيد "رومانسية الرواد" في "ولا قبلها من نساء ولا بعدها من أحد"|url=https://web.archive.org/web/20200419190730/http://www.alriyadh.com/831554|access-date=2021-04-12|website=web.archive.org}}</ref>

=== Translations ===

* The Treasury of Sanskrit Poetry, published by the [https://kalima.ae/ar/default.aspx Kalimah project] in 2012.<ref>{{Cite web|date=2020-04-19|title=خزانة الشعر السنسكريتي|url=https://web.archive.org/web/20200419190731/https://www.al-jazirah.com/culture/2012/20122012/tatb26.htm|access-date=2021-04-12|website=web.archive.org}}</ref>
* Gheyab Alasal (Absent honey): selection of poems, by American poet Mark Strand. Published by Dar Mayara in Tunisia in 2017.<ref>{{Cite web|date=2019-07-17|title=قراءة مارك ستراند عبر مختاراته العربية {{!}} مجلة الفيصل|url=https://web.archive.org/web/20190717064726/http://www.alfaisalmag.com/?p=15613|access-date=2021-04-12|website=web.archive.org}}</ref>
* Akhbar Alayyam (Chronicles), by the American poet and singer Bob Dylan. Published by Dar Alrewayat in 2018.<ref>{{Cite web|date=2020-04-19|title="روايات" تترجم اختيارات الجوائز الأدبية الغربية - صحيفة الاتحاد|url=https://web.archive.org/web/20200419190733/https://www.alittihad.ae/article/72297/2018/|access-date=2021-04-12|website=web.archive.org}}</ref>
