'''László Kálnoky''' ([[Eger]], September 5, 1912 – [[Budapest]], July 30, 1985) was a [[Hungarian people|Hungarian]] poet and literary translator.

He belonged to the third generation of [[Nyugat]]. His poetry was highly typical of [[pessimism]].
