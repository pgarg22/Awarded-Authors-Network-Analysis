'''Jeremi Przybora''' (12 December 1915 in [[Warsaw]] – 4 March 2004) was a [[Polish language|Polish]] poet, writer, actor and singer.  He created the TV-series "[[Kabaret Starszych Panów]]" (Elderly Gentlemen's Cabaret) with [[Jerzy Wasowski]] and performed [[ballad]]s and [[sung poetry]], a popular music genre in Poland.[[File:Jeremi Przybora grave.JPG|thumb|left|200px|Przybora's grave at the Calvinist Cemetery in Warsaw]]
