'''János Vajda''' (7 May 1827 – 18 January 1897) was a Hungarian poet and journalist. His first poetry was published to the public in ''[[Életképek]]'' in 1844.<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.babelmatrix.org/works/hu-all/Vajda_J%C3%A1nos/biography|title=Babel Web Anthology :: The page of Vajda János, English biography|work=babelmatrix.org|access-date=20 October 2015}}</ref>  He was a member of the [[Kisfaludy Society]].<ref>Szinnyei, József: ''[http://mek.oszk.hu/00300/00355/html/ABC16241/16294.htm Magyar írók élete és munkái XIV. (Telgárti–Zsutai)].'' Budapest. Hornyánszky. 1914.</ref>
