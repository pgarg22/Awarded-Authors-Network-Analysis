'''L. Dean James''' (born 1947) is an American [[fantasy]] and [[Horror fiction|horror]] writer. Her novel ''Winter Scream'', co-authored with [[Tamara Thorne|Chris Curry]], was nominated for the 1991 [[Bram Stoker Award]] for [[Bram Stoker Award for Best First Novel|Best First Novel]].<ref name="fant">{{cite web|url=http://www.fantasticfiction.co.uk/j/l-dean-james/|title=L Dean James|accessdate=2007-01-31|publisher=Fantastic Fiction}}</ref><ref>{{cite web |title=Winter Scream by Chris Curry and Lisa Dean |url=https://www.fantasticfiction.com/t/tamara-thorne/winter-scream.htm |website=www.fantasticfiction.com |access-date=24 April 2021}}</ref>
