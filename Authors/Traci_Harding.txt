'''Traci Harding''' is an Australian [[novelist]]. Her work blends fantasy, fact, esoteric belief, time travel and quantum physics, into adventurous romps through history, alternative dimensions, universes and states of consciousness.

She was born and raised in [[Carlingford, New South Wales|Carlingford]], a western suburb of [[Sydney]], [[New South Wales]].<ref name="Harpers">{{cite web|title=Traci Harding |publisher=HarperCollins Publishers New Zealand |url=http://www.harpercollins.co.nz/authors/author_profile.cfm?Author=HARDING__TRACI |accessdate=2007-07-28 |url-status=dead |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20070421092600/http://www.harpercollins.co.nz/authors/author_profile.cfm?Author=HARDING__TRACI |archivedate=April 21, 2007 }}</ref> Harding states that her early interests were, "music, boys, daydreaming and storytelling".<ref name="Traci">{{cite web |title= Traci Harding, About Traci |publisher= VoyagerOnLine |url= http://www.voyageronline.com.au/traciharding/about/index.htm |accessdate= 2007-07-28}}</ref>  After leaving school she first pursued an interest in music, and then worked in film studio management for several years where she wrote and developed film scripts, and later began writing novels.<ref name="Traci" />
