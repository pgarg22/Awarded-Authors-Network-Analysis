{{Nihongo|'''Yoshiki Tanaka'''|田中 芳樹|Tanaka Yoshiki|born October 22, 1952}} is a Japanese novelist.

{{refimprove|date=November 2021}}

== Early life and education ==
He was born in [[Hondo, Kumamoto|Hondo]], [[Kumamoto Prefecture]] and took his [[doctorate]] degree in Japanese Language and Literature in the Graduate School of [[Gakushuin University|Gakushūin University]] in Tokyo.

== Work ==
His major works include the fantasy novel series ''Arslan Senki'' (アルスラーン戦記), also known as ''[[The Heroic Legend of Arslan]]'', and the sci-fi [[space opera]] novel series entitled ''Ginga Eiyū Densetsu'' (銀河英雄伝説), also known as ''[[Legend of the Galactic Heroes]]'', both of which were adapted as [[anime]] and [[manga]]. His fantasy works also include the novel series ''[[Sohryuden: Legend of the Dragon Kings]]'' (創竜伝) that was also adapted as anime.

Tanaka is an avid fan of [[History of China|Chinese history]] and wrote some novels set in China. He also published two ''arranged''-translations of Chinese literature: "Sui Tang Yanyi" ([[:Zh:隋唐演義|隋唐演義]], "Stories of [[Sui Dynasty|Sui]] and [[Tang Dynasty|Tang]] Dynasties") and "[[General Yue Fei]]" ([[:Zh:說岳全傳|說岳全傳]], "Telling the Complete Biography of [[Yue Fei]]") as "Gakuhi-den" (岳飛伝, "The Story of Yue Fei"). He is also familiar with [[History of Iran|Persian history]], although ''Arslan Senki'' is not based on it and just based on his own thoughts and imaginations.

==Major works==
*''[[Legend of the Galactic Heroes]]'' (''Ginga Eiyū Densetsu'' 銀河英雄伝説, 1981&ndash;1987) Adapted into several formats by [[Kitty Films|Kitty Film Mitaka Studio]], [[Magic Bus (studio)|Magic Bus]], [[Artland (company)|Artland]], [[Mushi Production]], [[Shaft (company)|Shaft]], [[Madhouse (company)|MADHOUSE]], and [[Production I.G]].
*''[[The Heroic Legend of Arslan]]'' (''Arslan Senki'' アルスラーン戦記, Part One 1986&ndash;1990, Part Two 1991&ndash;2017) Adapted into an OVA series by [[J.C.Staff]] and Aubec, and a TV series by [[Liden Films]] and [[Sanzigen]].
*''[[Sohryuden: Legend of the Dragon Kings]]'' (''Sōryūden'' 創竜伝, 1987&ndash;''ongoing'') Adapted into an OVA series by Kitty Film Mitaka Studio.
*''[[Tytania]]'' (''Taitania'' タイタニア, 1988&ndash;2015) Adapted into a TV series by [[Artland (company)|Artland]].
*''[[Yakushiji Ryōko no Kaiki Jikenbo]]'' (薬師寺涼子の怪奇事件簿, 1996&ndash;2007) Adapted into a TV series by [[Doga Kobo]].
*''[[Ambition Waltz]]'' (''Yabou Enbukyoku'' 野望円舞曲, co-authored with [[Yuki Oginome]])
