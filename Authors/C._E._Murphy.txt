'''C. E. Murphy''' (born June 1, 1973) is an American-born author, based in Ireland, who writes in the [[fantasy]] and [[Romance novel|romance]] genres. She is the author of the ''Walker Papers'' series, ''The Negotiator Trilogy'', and the ''Inheritor's Cycle'' as well as ''The Strongbox Chronicles'' (written under a pseudonym). She has also written the [[graphic novel]] ''Take a Chance''.

==Biography==
C. E. Murphy is an [[Alaska]]n-born<ref name="Living">{{cite web|url=http://www.randomhouse.com/author/results.pperl?authorid=73073|title=Random House Author Spotlight|access-date=2010-09-20}}</ref> writer of [[fantasy novel]]s, [[short story|short stories]] and comic books. She has also written a [[romance novel]] trilogy under the pseudonym '''Cate Dermody''', which was her grandmother's maiden name.<ref name="Writing">{{cite web|url=http://fmwriters.com/Visionback/Issue27/Interview.htm|title=An Interview with C. E. Murphy|access-date=2010-09-20}}</ref><ref name="Pseudonym">{{cite web|url=http://www.fantasticfiction.co.uk/m/c-e-murphy/|title=Fantastic Fiction: C. E. Murphy|access-date=2010-09-20}}</ref>

She became an Irish citizen by grandparental descent in 2001,<ref name="Ownsite_cit2001">{{cite web |last1=Murphy |first1=C. E. |title=Failed! |url=http://mizkit.com/bookformats/audio/page/1136/ |website=Mizkit.com - Official website of author CE Murphy |access-date=18 January 2021 |date=22 August 2003}}</ref> moved to Ireland in 2005,<ref name="Ownsite_10yrs">{{cite web |last1=Murphy |first1=C. E. |title=Ten years in Ireland |url=http://mizkit.com/ten-years-in-ireland/ |website=Mizkit.com - Official website of author CE Murphy |access-date=18 January 2021 |date=1 December 2015}}</ref> and as of 2020, continued to live there with her husband and son.

Along with writing, she has worked at various points as a library volunteer, archival assistant, cannery worker, and web designer. However writing is her primary hobby.<ref name="About">{{cite web|url=http://mizkit.com/about/|title=Biography|date=March 19, 2006|access-date=2013-10-08}}</ref>
