'''Veronica Anne Roth''' (born August 19, 1988) is an American [[novelist]] and [[short story writer]], known for her [[The New York Times Best Seller list|bestselling]] [[Divergent trilogy|''Divergent'' trilogy]].

== Personal life ==
Veronica Roth was born on August 19, 1988 in [[New York City]], and was raised primarily in [[Barrington, Illinois]].<ref name=Wainwright>{{cite news |url=http://evanstonroundtable.com/main.asp?SectionID=4&SubSectionID=4&ArticleID=4435 |title='Divergent': A Bestseller Built in Evanston |last=Wainwright |first=Natalie |newspaper=Evanston Roundtable |date=July 5, 2011 |access-date=September 13, 2016}}</ref> Her mother, Barbara Ross, is a painter who resides in Barrington.<ref name=Wainwright /><ref name=Lizluby2013>{{cite web |url=https://365barrington.com/2013/10/06/248-veronica-roth-divergent-trilogy-interview/ |title=365BarringtonTV Interviews DIVERGENT Author Veronica Roth |author1=Lizluby |last2=Goetze |first2=Catherine |date=October 6, 2013 |access-date=October 23, 2013}}</ref> She is the youngest of three children. Her parents divorced when she was five years old, and her mother has since remarried to Frank Ross, a financial consultant for landscaping companies.<ref name=Wainwright /><ref name=ChiTribune2013 /> Her brother and sister live in the Chicago area.<ref name=Wainwright />

Roth is of German and Polish descent.<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.divergentfans.com/profiles/blogs/let-s-recap-the-hour-long-chat-with-veronica-roth-shall-we-insurg |title=Missed the Twitter Chat? Not to Worry~Full Transcript of Veronica Roth's Q & A Session [5/1] #InsurgentDay |author=First Jumper |date=May 1, 2012 |access-date=September 15, 2014 |publisher=Divergent Fans (unofficial fan site) |url-status=dead |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20140315011620/http://www.divergentfans.com/profiles/blogs/let-s-recap-the-hour-long-chat-with-veronica-roth-shall-we-insurg |archive-date=March 15, 2014 }}</ref><ref>{{cite news |url=http://www.irishtimes.com/culture/film/roth-s-child-divergent-author-talks-1.1745951 |title=Roth's child: Divergent author talks |newspaper=The Irish Times |date=April 4, 2014 |author=Clarke, Donald |access-date=April 28, 2015}}</ref> Roth says of her father: "He had a job, and worked far away. Now I have a good relationship with my stepdad."<ref>{{cite news |url=https://www.independent.co.uk/arts-entertainment/books/features/i-dont-want-smut-on-the-page-divergent-author-veronica-roth-on-sex-and-teen-fiction-9035852.html |title='I don't want smut on the page': Divergent author Veronica Roth on sex and teen fiction |author=Kidd, James |date=January 5, 2014 |newspaper=[[The Independent]] |access-date=September 15, 2014}}</ref> Her maternal grandparents were concentration camp survivors, whose religious convictions pushed her mother away from religion. Roth learned about [[Christianity]] by attending a Christian Bible study during her high school years, and has stayed with it.<ref name=ChiTribune2013 /><ref>{{cite web |url=http://veronicarothbooks.blogspot.com/2010/09/christian-take-on-banning-speak.html |title=Christian Take on Banning SPEAK |publisher=VeronicaRothBooks |work=blogspot |author=Roth, Veronica |date=29 September 2010 |access-date=March 29, 2014}}</ref>

Roth went to Grove Avenue Elementary School, Prairie Middle School, and [[Barrington High School (Lake County, Illinois)|Barrington High School]].<ref name=Lizluby2013 /> After one year at [[Carleton College]], she transferred to [[Northwestern University]] for its creative writing program.<ref name=ChiTribune2013 /><ref name=Leopold2010>{{cite web |url=http://www.northwestern.edu/newscenter/stories/2010/05/roth.html |author=Leopold, Wendy |date=May 5, 2010 |title=A Book Contract Already! Weinberg undergraduate gets a three-book deal from HarperCollins |publisher=Northwestern University News Center |access-date=August 29, 2013}}</ref>

Roth married photographer Nelson Fitch in 2011.<ref name=Bock>{{cite web |last=Bock|first=Cathy and David |title=Veronica and Nelson Married |url=http://cathydavidphoto.com/chicago-photographer-weddings/evanston-illinois-wedding-photography-womens-club |publisher=Cathy and David Photographers}}</ref> They reside in the Chicago area.<ref name=ChiTribune2013 />

== Career ==
Roth wrote her first book, ''[[Divergent (novel)|Divergent]]'', while on winter break in her senior year at [[Northwestern University]], and found an agent by the following March.<ref name=ChiTribune2013>{{cite news |url=http://articles.chicagotribune.com/2013-10-21/entertainment/chi-veronica-roth-profile-20131021_1_veronica-roth-allegiant-anderson |title=The next YA superstar? 12, 2011 |access-date=April 12, 2012 |author=Dominus, Susan}}</ref><ref name=Crowder2011>{{cite news |url=http://www.chicagotribune.com/lifestyles/books/ct-books-0423-veronica-roth-20110422-story.html Roth sold the film rights to ''[[The Divergent Series]]'' to [[Summit Entertainment]].<ref name=Crowder2011 /><ref name="Screencrave.com">{{cite web |url=http://screencrave.com/2011-07-21/comic-con-2011-veronica-roth-from-divergent-press-conference/ |title=Comic-Con 2011: Veronica Roth from Divergent Press Conference |publisher=Screencrave.com |author=Elfman, Mali |date=July 21, 2011 |access-date=March 10, 2012 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20111208130947/http://screencrave.com/2011-07-21/comic-con-2011-veronica-roth-from-divergent-press-conference/ |archive-date=December 8, 2011 |url-status=dead |df=mdy-all }}</ref><ref name="Kung, Michelle">{{cite news |url=https://blogs.wsj.com/speakeasy/2011/07/23/divergent-author-veronica-roth-on-her-film-adaptation/ |author=Kung, Michelle |title='Divergent' Author Veronica Roth on Her Film Adaptation |newspaper=The Wall Street Journal |date=July 23, 2011 | access-date=13 April 2012 }}</ref> Filming of ''[[Divergent (film)|Divergent]]'', the adaptation of the first book in the series, was started in April 2013, and the film was released in March 2014. On March 21, 2014, Lionsgate officially greenlit the [[The Divergent Series: Insurgent|film adaptation]] of ''[[Insurgent (novel)|Insurgent]]''.<ref name="debut">{{cite web|url=https://www.thewrap.com/divergent-box-office/ |title='Divergent' Debuts to $4.9 Million, Lionsgate Greenlights 'Insurgent' |author=Lang, Brent |access-date=March 22, 2014}}</ref><ref>{{cite news |url=http://articles.chicagotribune.com/2014-03-06/entertainment/chi-insurgent-filming-atlanta-divergent-chicago-20140306_1_dystopian-chicago-film-incentive-veronica-roth |title='Divergent' sequel will film in Atlanta, but what about Chicago? |author=Gomez, Luis |date=March 6, 2014 |access-date=April 26, 2014 |newspaper=Chicago Tribune }}</ref> The film was released on March 20, 2015.<ref>{{cite journal |url=http://time.com/3753522/insurgent-box-office/ |title=''Insurgent'' Tops Weekend Box Office But Misses ''Divergent'' Mark |date=March 22, 2015 |author=Linshi, Jack |journal=Time Magazine |access-date=March 22, 2015}}</ref> On April 11, 2014, [[Summit Entertainment]] announced that the third book, ''[[Allegiant (novel)|Allegiant]]'', would be split into two films, part 1 and part 2. Former Part 1 is called ''[[The Divergent Series: Allegiant]]'', and was released on March 18, 2016, and former Part 2 is named ''The Divergent Series: Ascendant'', and was scheduled to be released on March 24, 2017.<ref>{{cite news |url=http://www.ew.com/article/2015/09/10/divergent-allegiant-new-titles |title=The last two Divergent movies have titles: Allegiant and Ascendant |author=Coggan, Devan |newspaper=Entertainment Weekly |date=September 10, 2015 |access-date=November 2, 2015}}</ref><ref>{{cite news |title=Lionsgate Splitting Third 'Divergent' Book 'Allegiant' into Two Films |url=https://variety.com/2014/film/news/lionsgate-splitting-third-divergent-book-allegiant-into-two-films-1201155988/ |author=McNary, Dave |newspaper=Variety |date=April 11, 2014 |access-date=November 2, 2015}}</ref> The theatrical release for ''Ascendant'' was cancelled in favor of it being a television film and spinoff series.<ref>{{cite news |url=https://variety.com/2016/tv/news/lionsgate-bullish-outlook-divergent-tv-spinoff-1201830926/|title=Lionsgate Offers Confident Outlook for 'Divergent' TV Spinoff: 'There's a Tremendous Fandom' |last=McNary|first=Dave |date=August 4, 2016 |newspaper=Variety |access-date=February 6, 2019}}</ref> The television film and spinoff series were later cancelled.<ref>{{cite news |url=https://www.buzzfeednews.com/article/kateaurthur/divergent-franchise-disaster |title=How "Divergent" Turned Into A Disaster |last=Arthur |first=Kate |website=BuzzFeed News |date=December 18, 2018 |access-date=February 6, 2019}}</ref>

Roth has written four short stories from character Tobias Eaton's point of view. ''The Transfer'' is the first of four short stories, which tells some of Tobias's life prior to ''Divergent''. It was released on September 3, 2013.<ref>{{cite web |url=http://www.harperteen.com/books/Transfer-Divergent-Story-Veronica-Roth/?isbn=9780062285638 |title=The Transfer: A Divergent Story |publisher=HarperCollins |access-date=October 23, 2013}}</ref> The second story is titled ''The Initiate''.<ref>{{cite web |url=http://www.harperteen.com/books/Initiate-Divergent-Story-Unabridged/?isbn=9780062316134?AA=index_authorIntro_37588 |title=The Initiate: A Divergent Story unabridged |access-date=October 23, 2013 |publisher=HarperCollins}}</ref> The stories are sold separately as e-books and also bound together under the title ''Four: A Divergent Story Collection''.<ref>{{cite web |url=http://veronicarothbooks.blogspot.com/2013/12/news-about-four-stories_7.html |title=News About the "Four" Stories |author=Roth, Veronica |date=December 7, 2013 |access-date=January 8, 2014 }}</ref> The last two short stories of Tobias's stories are titled ''The Son'' and ''The Traitor''.<ref>{{cite web |url=http://www.harperteen.com/books/Four-Divergent-Story-Collection-Unabridged/?isbn=9780062316165 |title=Four |publisher=HarperCollins |access-date=October 23, 2013}}</ref> The story collection was published in several forms in July 2014.<ref>{{cite web |url=http://www.fantasticfiction.co.uk/r/veronica-roth/four-divergent-story-collection.htm |title=Four: A Divergent Story Collection |publisher=Fantastic Fiction |access-date=October 12, 2014}}</ref> The novella ''Free Four: Tobias Tells the Story'' chapter thirteen of ''Divergent'' in Tobias's point of view, and was released for Kindle in 2012 in the United States and in 2013 in the UK.<ref>{{cite web |url=http://www.fantasticfiction.co.uk/r/veronica-roth/free-four.htm |title=Free Four |publisher=Fantastic Fiction |access-date=November 2, 2015}}</ref>

Publisher HarperCollins announced a two-book deal with Roth for publication of two young adult novels. The first book, ''[[Carve the Mark]]'', was published on January 17, 2017<ref>{{cite web |title=HarperCollins Publishers Announces Two-Book Deal with Veronica Roth |url=http://corporate.harpercollins.com/us/press-releases/426/HARPERCOLLINS%20PUBLISHERS%20ANNOUNCES%20TWO-BOOK%20DEAL%20WITH%20VERONICA%20ROTH |publisher=HarperCollins |date=March 2, 2015 |access-date=December 7, 2015}}</ref><ref>{{cite news |url=http://www.ew.com/article/2016/05/05/veronica-roth-carve-the-mark|title=Veronica Roth reveals Carve the Mark, first book in sci-fi duology|last=Biedenharn|first=Isabella |newspaper=Entertainment Weekly|date=May 5, 2016|access-date=September 18, 2016}}</ref> and the sequel, titled ''The Fates Divide'', was released on April 10, 2018.<ref>{{cite news|url=https://chicago.suntimes.com/entertainment/veronica-roth-dots-her-latest-novel-with-little-pieces-of-chicago/|title=Veronica Roth dots new novel 'The Fates Divide' with 'little pieces' of Chicago|last=Svachula|first=Amanda|date=April 20, 2018|newspaper=Chicago Sun-Times|access-date=May 1, 2018}}</ref> An epilogue to ''Divergent'', titled ''We Can Be Mended'', was announced in December 2016. It could either be purchased independently or included as an extra with a ''Carve the Mark'' pre-order.<ref name=Vowelor2016 />

In October 2018, [[HarperCollins]] announced another two-book deal with Roth. The first is a collection of short stories tiled ''[[The End and Other Beginnings: Stories]]'' which take place in a future with advanced technology. One of the stories in the collections, ''Inertia'', has been optioned for film adaptation by [[Fox 2000 Pictures]].<ref>{{cite web |url=https://www.publishersweekly.com/pw/by-topic/childrens/childrens-authors/article/78221-harpercollins-acquires-two-books-by-veronica-roth.html |title=HarperCollins Acquires Two Books by Veronica Roth |last=Kantor |first=Emma |date=October 4, 2018 |website=Publishers Weekly |access-date=October 1, 2019}}</ref>  

[[John Joseph Adams Books]] acquired Roth's book ''Chosen Ones'' for publication on April 6, 2020.<ref>{{cite web |url=http://www.johnjosephadams.com/2018/08/02/news-the-chosen-one-by-veronica-roth-acquired-for-john-joseph-adams-books-houghton-mifflin-harcourt/ |title=NEWS: THE CHOSEN ONE by Veronica Roth Acquired for John Joseph Adams Books (Houghton Mifflin Harcourt) |date=August 2, 2018 |website=John Joseph Adams |access-date=October 1, 2019}}</ref><ref>{{cite web |url=https://ew.com/books/2019/09/30/veronica-roth-chosen-ones-preview/ |title=Chosen Ones: See an exclusive preview of Veronica Roth's thrilling adult debut |last=Canfield |first=David |date=September 30, 2019 |website=EW.com |access-date=October 1, 2019}}</ref>
