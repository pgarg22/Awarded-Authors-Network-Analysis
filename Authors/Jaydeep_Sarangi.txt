'''Jaydeep Sarangi''' (Bengali: জয়দীপ ষড়ঙ্গী) is a bilingual writer, poet, and critic.

==Early life==
Jaydeep Sarangi was born on 11 December 1973 in [[Jhargram]], [[West Bengal]].
