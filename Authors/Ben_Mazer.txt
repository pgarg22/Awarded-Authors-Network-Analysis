{{short description|American poet and editor (born 1964)}}
'''Ben Mazer''' (born 1964 in [[New York City]]) is an [[American poet]] and editor.

== Life ==

Mazer was born in New York City and raised in the [[Cambridge, Massachusetts]] area. He studied under [[Seamus Heaney]] and [[William Alfred]] at [[Harvard University]]. Following graduation, he entered the [[Editorial Institute]] at [[Boston University]] to focus on textual scholarship.<ref>{{Cite web|url=http://www.bu.edu/clarion/14/interview-with-ben-mazer.htm|title=Clarion Magazine at Boston University}}</ref>

Mazer is the editor of the ''Battersea Review''. He lives in Cambridge.
