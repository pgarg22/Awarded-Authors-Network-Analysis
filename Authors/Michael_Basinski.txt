'''Michael Basinski''' (born 1950 in [[Buffalo, New York]]) is an American text, visual and sound [[Poetry|poet]]. He was the curator of The Poetry Collection of the University Libraries, [[University at Buffalo, The State University of New York|State University of New York at Buffalo]]. He performs as a solo poet and with the performance/sound ensemble, Bufffluxus.<ref>Van Maele, Francis. "Michael Basinki "Welcome To The Alphabet"" http://www.redfoxpress.com/dada-basinski.html. September 6, 2009.</ref>
